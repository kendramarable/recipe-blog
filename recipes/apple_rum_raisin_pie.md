## Apple Rum Raisin Pie

#### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 55 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients: Crust
 
- 1 cup graham crackers
- ¾ cup ground pecans
- 6 tablespoons butter

## Ingredients: Topping

- ½ cup all-purpose flour
- ¼ cup light brown sugar
- ¼ cup (½ stick) butter
- ¾ cup chopped pecans

## Ingredients: Filling

- ⅔ cup sour cream
- ½ cup packed light brown sugar
- 1 egg
- 2 teaspoons rum extract
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 3 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 pound Granny Smith apples, peeled and cut into thin slices
- ½ cup seedless raisins


## Instructions: Crust

1. **Combine** crumbs, pecans, and butter

2. **Press into** a 9-inch pan

3. **Refrigerate**


## Instructions: Topping

1. **Blend** flour, sugar, and butter with fingers until crumbly

2. **Stir in** pecans


## Instructions: Filling

1. **Whisk** sour cream, sugar, egg, rum extract, vanilla, flour, and salt in a bowl

2. **Add** apples and raisins

3. **Spoon** into a pie pan; smooth out the top

4. **Sprinkle** with topping

5. **Bake** at 375 degrees for 50-55 minutes

6. **Cover** with foil to prevent browning too quickly

7. **Serve** at room temperature
