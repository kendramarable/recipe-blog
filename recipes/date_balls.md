# Date Balls

### Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Makes 18 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 stick margarine
- 1 cup sugar
- 8 ounces dates, chopped
- 2 cups Rice Krispies
- 1 cup chopped nuts
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- coconut or confectioners sugar

## Instructions

1. **Mix** the margarine, sugar, and dates

2. **Cover** over low heat until sugar melts and mixture is thick

3. **Remove** from heat and let cool

4. **Add** the Rice Krispies, chopped nuts, and vanilla

5. **Shape** into balls and roll in coconut or confectioners sugar
