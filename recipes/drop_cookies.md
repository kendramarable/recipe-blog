# Drop Cookies

#### Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Makes 24 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 ½ cups flour
- ⅔ cup shortening
- ¾ cup sugar
- ½ teaspoon vanilla
- 1 egg
- 4 teaspoons milk

## Instructions

1. **Cream** shortening, sugar, and vanilla

2. **Add** egg, then beat

4. **Add** milk and flour

5. **Bake** at 375 degrees for 12-15 minutes
