#  Crunchy Coffee Cake

#### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 40 minutes | Makes 16 Servings

## Ingredients: Cake
 
- ¾ cup sugar
- 2 cups all-purpose flour, sifted
- 2 teaspoons double-acting baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon nutmeg
- ⅓ cup shortening
- 1 cup milk
- 2 eggs

## Ingredients: Topping

- ⅔ cup light brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup nut meats, chopped


## Instructions: Cake

1. **Combine and sift together** the first six (dry) ingredients

2. **Add** shortening and milk; beat 1 ½ minutes (or 225 strokes)

3. **Add** eggs; beat another 1 ½ minutes

4. **Pour** one-third of batter into pan, with the bottom greased (do not grease the sides)

5. **Sprinkle** half the topping over the batter; cover with remaining batter; sprinkle with remaining topping

6. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 35 to 40 minutes; serve warm

## Instructions: Topping

1. **Combine** brown sugar and flour

2. **Cut in** butter to resemble corn meal

3. **Add** chopped nuts

4. **Chill** until ready to use
