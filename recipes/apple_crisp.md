# Apple Crisp

### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 50 minutes | Makes 6 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 6 apples, peeled and sliced
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup flour
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- butter

## Instructions

1. **Mix** sugar and flour together in a bowl

2. **Mix** in apples

3. **Place** in a baking dish

4. **Sprinkle** cinnamon on top of the mixture

5. **Shave** butter over the top

6. **Bake** at 350 degrees until crispy, about 40-50 minutes
