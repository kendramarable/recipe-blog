# Angel Biscuits

### Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Makes 24 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 packages dry yeast
- 4 tablespoons lukewarm water
- 5 cups sifted flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 3 teaspoons baking flour
- 4 tablespoons sugar
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 cup shortening
- 2 cups buttermilk

## Instructions

1. **Dissolve** yeast in lukewarm water

2. **Sift** the dry ingredients in a large bowl, then cut in shortening

3. **Add** yeast and buttermilk to dry mixture

4. **Knead** about 20 times, enough to hold the dough together

5. **Roll and cut** into biscuit shapes

6. **Optional:** biscuits may be spread with butter and folded in half

7. **Bake** on an ungreased cookie sheet in a 400 degree oven for 15-20 minutes

8. **Note:** unused dough may be stored in refrigerator
