## Guacamole Salad

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 0 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 4-ounce can taco sauce
- 2 cups mashed avocados
- 1 teaspoon lemon juice
- garlic salt
- lettuce
- tostadas or corn chips


## Instructions

1. **Mash up** ripe avocados

2. **Add** taco sauce, garlic salt, and lemon juice

3. **Mix** well

4. **Serve** on bed of lettuce with tostadas or corn chips
