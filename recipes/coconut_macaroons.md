## Coconut Macaroons

#### Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Makes 30 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- ½ can (⅔ cup) sweetened condensed milk
- 3 cups shredded coconut
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Instructions

1. **Blend** together condensed milk, coconut, and vanilla

2. **Drop** by teaspoonfuls, about 1 inch apart, onto well-greased baking sheet

3. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 8-10 minutes, or until delicate brown

4. **Remove** from baking sheet at once


###### *source: grandma's recipe, originally from Borden*
