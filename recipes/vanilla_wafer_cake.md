## Vanilla Wafer Cake

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 90 minutes | Makes 12 Servings

## Ingredients: Cake
 
- 1 12-ounce package vanilla wafers, finely crushed
- 2 cups flaked coconut
- 2 cups sugar
- 6 eggs
- 2 sticks butter or margarine, melted
- 1 cup nuts, broken
- ½ cup milk

## Ingredients: Icing

- 10 tablespoons sugar
- 10 tablespoons light cream or evaporated milk
- 4 tablespoons butter


## Instructions: Cake

1. **Mix** all ingredients

2. **Bake** in greased pan at 300 degrees for 1 ½ hours

3. **Drizzle** with icing


## Instructions: Icing

1. **Combine** all ingredients

2. **Boil** for 5 minutes, then let cool
