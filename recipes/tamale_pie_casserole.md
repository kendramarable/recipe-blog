# Tamale Pie Casserole

#### Prep Time: 25 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 pound ground beef
- 1 15-ounce can tamales
- 1 8-ounce can tomato sauce
- ½ cup chopped onion
- ½ cup cheddar cheese, shredded
- 1 teaspoon oregano
- 1 teaspoon salt


## Instructions

1. **Preheat** oven to 375 degrees

2. **Brown** ground beef and onion over medium heat; drain

3. **Remove** corn husks from tamales, saving chili gravy

4. **Cut** tamales into pieces

5. **Add** tamales, chili gravy, tomato sauce, salt, and oregano to meat mixture

6. **Pour** into 1 ½ quart casserole dish

7. **Sprinkle** with cheese

8. **Bake** for 15 minutes


###### *source: grandma's recipe, originally from "Casseroles from Old El Paso"*
