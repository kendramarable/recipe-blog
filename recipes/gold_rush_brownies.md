#  Gold Rush Brownies

### Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 40 minutes | Makes 16 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 cups firmly packed coarse graham cracker crumbs (about 18 crackers)
- 1 package (6 ounces) semi-sweet chocolate pieces
- ½ cup coarsely chopped nut meats
- 1 can (1 ⅓ cups) sweetened condensed milk

## Instructions

1. **Mix** together graham cracker crumbs, chocolate pieces, and nut meats

2. **Blend in** condensed milk

3. **Pour** into 8x8x2 inch square pan which has been greased, bottom lined with waxed paper, and greased again

4. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 40 minutes, or until top is golden brown


*source: grandma's recipe, originally from Borden*
