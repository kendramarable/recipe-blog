## Coconut Thumbprint Cookies

#### Prep Time: 30 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Makes Several Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 cup butter or margarine
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup sifted confectioners sugar
- 1 teaspoon almond extract (optional)
- 2 cups unsifted cake flour
- 1 cup sweetened coconut
- ¾ cup ground or finely chopped blanched almonds (optional)
- jam or preserves


## Instructions

1. **Cream** butter with salt

2. **Gradually beat** in sugar

3. **Blend in** almond extract

4. **Add** flour a small amount at a time, mixing well after each addition

5. **Stir in** coconut and almonds

6. **Chill** dough

7. **Shape** dough into walnut-sized balls

8. **Place** on ungreased baking sheets

10. **Bake** at 325 degrees for 8 minutes

11. **Remove** from oven and make depression in center of each ball with thumb or measuring spoon

12. **Return** to oven, baking 12 to 15 minutes longer, or just until lightly browned

13. **Cool** cookies on sheets

14. **Fill** each cookie with 1 teaspoon jam

15. **Sprinkle** with additional coconut, if desired
