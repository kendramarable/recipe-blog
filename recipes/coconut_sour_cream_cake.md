#  Coconut Sour Cream Cake

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes | Makes 12 Servings

## Ingredients: Cake
 
- 1 box white cake mix
- 1 carton (8 ounces) sour cream
- ¼ cup oil
- 3 eggs
- 1 can (8 ½ ounces) cream of coconut

## Ingredients: Coconut Icing

- 8 ounces cream cheese
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 1 can flaked coconut
- 1 box powdered sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla

## Instructions: Cake

1. **Mix** all ingredients with an electric mixer for as long as the cake mix directs

2. **Place** mixture in a 9x12 inch pan

3. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 30 minutes

## Instructions: Coconut Icing

1. **Blend** sugar into softened cream cheese

2. **Work in** the milk and vanilla

3. **Ice** the cake, then sprinkle coconut on top
