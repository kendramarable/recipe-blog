## Holiday Cookies

#### Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Makes 24 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup shortening
- 2 cups sugar
- ½ cup milk
- 3 ½ cups flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 ½ cup pecans
- 2 cups cherry halves
- 2 cups chopped dates


## Instructions

1. **Mix** all ingredients

2. **Bake** at 400 degrees for 8-10 minutes
