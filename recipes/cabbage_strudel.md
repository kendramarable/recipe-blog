# Cabbage Strudel

### Prep Time: 25 minutes | Cook Time: 35 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 head cabbage, finely shredded
- 1 stick margarine
- salt
- pepper
- sour cream
- 1 cup fine dry breadcrumbs
- 1 package phyllo dough


## Instructions

1. **Cook** cabbage in margarine, stirring often, until tender (about 15 minutes)

2. **Add** salt and pepper to taste; set aside to cool

3. **Spread** phyllo dough with sour cream, then sprinkle with breadcrumbs

4. **Top** with cabbage, making layers

5. **Roll** and bake in oven at 350 degrees until golden brown, about 35 minutes

6. **Slice** and serve warm
