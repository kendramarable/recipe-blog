## Italian Cream Cake

#### Prep Time: 60 minutes | Cook Time: 25 minutes | Makes 20 Servings

## Ingredients: Cake
 
- 2 cups sugar
- ½ cup light butter
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 1 cup low-fat buttermilk
- ½ cup chopped pecans
- 1 teaspoon butter extract
- 1 teaspoon coconut extract
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 6 egg whites, room temperature
- vegetable cooking spray
- optional: sugared kumquats, orange rind strips, kumquat leaves

## Ingredients: Cream Cheese Icing

- 1 tablespoon light butter
- 1 8-ounce package neufchatel cheese
- 1 1-pound package powdered sugar, sifted
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract


## Instructions: Cake

1. **Coat** bottom of 3 9-inch round cake pans with cooking spray (do not coat sides of pans)

2. **Line** bottoms of pans with wax paper

3. **Coat** wax paper with cooking spray, dust with flour, and set aside

4. **Combine** sugar and butter in a large bowl

5. **Beat** at medium speed of a mixer until well-blended

6. **Add** egg yolks, 1 at a time, beating well after each addition

7. **Combine** flour and baking soda; stir well

8. **Add** flour mixture to creamed mixture alternatively with buttermilk, beginning and ending with flour mixture

9. **Stir** in pecans and extracts

10. **Beat** egg whites at high speed of a mixer until stiff peaks form (do not overbeat)

11. **Fold** egg whites into batter

12. **Pour** batter into prepared pans

13. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 23 minutes

14. **Let cool** in pans for 5 minutes on a wire rack

15. **Loosen** cake layers from sides of pans using a narrow metal spatula, and turn out onto wire racks

16. **Peel off** wax paper, and let cool completely

17. **Place** one cake layer on a place, and spread with ⅔ cup cream cheese icing

18. **Top** with another cake layer

19. **Repeat** with another ⅔ cup icing and remaining layer, ending with cake

20. **Spread** remaining icing over cake

21. **Garnish** with kumquats, orange rind, and kumquat leaves, if desired

## Instructions: Cream Cheese Icing

1. **Cream** butter and cheese at high speed of a mixer until fluffy

2. **Add** sugar

3. **Beat** at low speed until well-blended

4. **Add** vanilla

5. **Beat** well

6. **Cover** and chill to be used on cake
