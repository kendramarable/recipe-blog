# Magic Cookie Bars

### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes | Makes 24 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- ½ cup butter or margarine
- 1 ½ cups graham cracker crumbs
- 1 can sweetened condensed milk
- 1 6-ounce package chocolate chips
- 1 3 ½-ounce can flaked coconut
- 1 cup chopped nuts

## Instructions

1. **Preheat** oven to 350 degrees

2. **Melt** margarine in a 13x9 inch pan

3. **Sprinkle** graham cracker crumbs over margarine

4. **Mix together** and press in pan

5. **Pour** sweetened condensed milk over crumbs

6. **Top** evenly with the remaining ingredients

7. **Press down** firmly

8. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 25-30 minutes
