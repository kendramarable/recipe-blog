## Chili Relleno Corn Casserole

#### Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 4-ounce cans whole green chili
- 1 6-ounce can evaporated milk
- 2 15-ounce cans cream-style corn
- 2 eggs
- Â½ pound cheddar or longhorn cheese


## Instructions

1. **Cut** cheese into strips and stuff green chilies generously with the cheese

2. **Line** the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish with the cheese-stuffed chilies

3. **Combine** corn, eggs, and evaporated milk in a large bowl; mix together thoroughly

4. **Pour** corn mixture over chilies and cheese

5. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 30 minutes
