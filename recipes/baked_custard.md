#  Baked Custard

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 60 minutes | Makes 6 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- ½ can (⅔ cup) sweetened condensed milk
- 2 cups hot water
- 3 eggs, slightly beaten
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- nutmeg

## Instructions

1. **Combine** condensed milk and hot water

2. **Stir** gradually into eggs

3. **Stir** in salt and vanilla

4. **Pour** into greased 1-quart casserole or baking dish (or 6 individual custard cups)

5. **Sprinkle** top with nutmeg

6. **Place** in shallow pan filled with hot water to depth of one inch

7. **Bake** in oven at 325 degrees for 1 hour, or until a knife inserted near the center comes out clean

8. **Chill** before serving
