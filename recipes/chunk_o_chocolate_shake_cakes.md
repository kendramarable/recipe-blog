#  Chunk o' Chocolate Shake Cakes

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 25 minutes | Makes 18 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- ½ cup butter, softened
- ½ cup boiling water
- ½ cup sugar
- ¾ cup instant chocolate malted milk powder
- 1 cup all-purpose flour
- 1 ½ teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 eggs, beaten
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 1-ounce envelopes pre-melted unsweetened chocolate
- 1 9 ¾-inch milk chocolate candy bar, cut into ½ inch pieces

## Instructions

1. **Measure** butter and water into large (48 ounce) jar or shaker; shake to melt butter

2. **Add** remaining ingredients, reserving ½ cup candy pieces

3. **Cover** tightly and shake about 10 times

4. **Stir** well; shake vigorously until well-blended

5. **Fill** paper baking cups in muffin pans two-thirds full

6. **Sprinkle** reserved candy over cupcakes

7. **Bake** at 375 degrees for 20 to 25 minutes

8. **Cool** and drizzle with confectioners sugar glaze, if desired
