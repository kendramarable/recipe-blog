# Raisin Oatmeal Cookies

### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 12 minutes | Makes 20 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 cup shortening
- 1 ½ cups brown sugar, packed
- 2 eggs
- ½ cup milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- 2 teaspoons baking powder
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- 2 ½ cups rolled oats
- ½ cup raisins
- ½ cup nuts


## Instructions

1. **Cream** shortening, eggs, and sugar

2. **Add** milk and vanilla, then blend

3. **Sift** dry ingredients together, then add to mixture

4. **Mix in** the oats, raisins, and nuts

5. **Drop** dough by teaspoons, 2 inches apart on greased cookie sheet

6. **Bake** at 400 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes
