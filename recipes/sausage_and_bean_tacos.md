#  Sausage and Bean Tacos

### Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 15 Â½ ounce can refritos con chorizos
- 12 fully cooked taco shells
- taco sauce
- shredded cheese
- shredded lettuce

## Instructions

1. **Heat** refritos con chorizos in a small saucepan over medium heat until steaming

2. **Heat** taco shells in oven at 200 degrees for 5 minutes

3. **Spoon** refritos con chorizos into shells

4. **Fill** shells with lettuce and cheese

5. **Top** with taco sauce
