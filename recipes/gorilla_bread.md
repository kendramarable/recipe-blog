## Gorilla Bread

#### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 60 minutes | Makes 10 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 10-count cans of biscuits
- 1 8-ounce brick of cream cheese
- 1 ½ cups crushed walnuts or pecans
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 10 teaspoons cinnamon sugar
- ½ cup butter, melted

## Instructions

1. **Spray** a bundt pan with cooking spray

2. **Cut** cream cheese into 20 cubes 

3. **Flatten** biscuits

4. **Place** a cube of cream cheese on each biscuit

5. **Sprinkle** ½ teaspoon cinnamon sugar on each biscuit

6. **Close up** each biscuit into a roll

7. **Place** a layer of 10 biscuits into the bundt pan

8. **Drizzle** with half the melted butter and brown sugar

9. **Cover** with half the crushed walnuts or pecans

10. **Place** a second layer of biscuits on top

11. **Cover** with the remaining butter, brown sugar, and nuts

12. **Bake** at 350 degrees for about 30 minutes
