# Salmon-Pesto Focaccia Sandwiches

#### Prep Time: 30 minutes | Cook Time: 0 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 14 ¾-ounce can or 2 7 ½-ounce cans Alaska canned salmon, drained and flaked
- ½ cup prepared pesto
- ½ cup sweet or red onion, finely chopped
- ⅓ cup oil packed sun dried tomatoes, chopped and drained
- 3 tablespoons reduced fat mayonnaise
- 2 teaspoons lemon peel, finely shredded
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 8-inch round focaccia bread, or 1 12-inch French bread loaf
- Romaine lettuce leaves

## Instructions

1. **Place** salmon in a medium bowl

2. **Add** pesto, onion, tomatoes, mayonnaise, lemon peel, and salt

3. **Mix** well

4. **Split** bread in half

5. **Layer** lettuce leaves on bottom half of bread

6. **Top** with salmon mixture and top of bread

7. **Cut** into four pieces

8. **Serve** immediately, or cover with plastic wrap and refrigerate up to 6 hours before serving
