# Chili Meat

#### Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 50 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 Â½ pounds lean pork, cut into 1-inch cubes
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 tablespoons reserved pork drippings
- 2 15-ounce cans mild enchilada sauce
- 1 tablespoon chili powder
- 1 garlic clove, sliced
- 2 cups water
- salt
- pepper



## Instructions

1. **Brown** the pork in the melted pork fat in a heavy skillet on medium heat

2. **Salt and pepper** the meat to taste after browning

3. **Stir** in the flour to coat the meat

4. **Add** water to cover the meat and simmer (covered) until fork tender, about 30 minutes

5. **Add** the enchilada sauce, chili powder, and garlic

6. **Simmer** about 20 minutes longer
