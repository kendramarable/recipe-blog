# Chinese Pepper Steak

### Prep Time: 50 minutes | Cook Time: 60 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 pound boneless chuck roast, cut into 1-inch strips
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ¼ cup oil
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 cup + ½ cup water
- 1 cup green pepper strips
- 1 large onion, sliced
- ½ cup celery
- 2 tomatoes, cut into wedges
- 1 tablespoon corn starch
- hot cooked rice
- sliced mushrooms


## Instructions

1. **Brown** meat quickly in hot oil

2. **Add** soy sauce, garlic, and ½ cup water

3. **Bring** to a boil

4. **Cover and simmer** for about 45 minutes

5. **Add** green pepper, onion, celery, and mushrooms

6. **Cover and simmer** until vegetables are crisp and tender

7. **Add** tomato wedges; toss lightly

8. **Dissolve** corn starch in 1 cup of water, then add to meat mixture

9. **Cook** over medium heat, stirring until boiling and thickened

10. **Serve** over hot rice
