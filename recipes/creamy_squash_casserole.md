# Creamy Squash Casserole

#### Prep Time: 30 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 ½ pounds yellow squash, sliced
- 1 can cream of chicken soup
- 1 carton sour cream
- 1 jar pimento, drained and sliced
- 1 can water chestnuts, thinly sliced
- 2 medium onions, chopped
- 1 stick of butter
- ½ package herbed stuffing mix


## Instructions

1. **Cook** squash in water for 12-15 minutes, or until tender

2. **Add** squash to the rest of the ingredients except butter and stuffing mix; mix well

3. **Melt** stick of butter in frying pan and add stuffing mix

4. **Mix** well

5. **Pack** into bottom of 2-quart baking dish, reserving some stuffing for the top

6. **Pour** squash mixture over stuffing; sprinkle remaining stuffing over top

7. **Put** rest of butter on top of casserole

8. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 30 minutes
