# Spanish Rice

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Makes 6 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 cup rice
- 2 cups liquid tomato sauce
- Â¼ cup onion
- bacon drippings
- salt and pepper to taste

## Instructions

1. **Soften** bacon drippings in a skillet

2. **Add** rice and tomato sauce

3. **Add** salt and pepper to taste

4. **Bring** to a boil

5. **Put** cover on and simmer for 20 minutes or until done
