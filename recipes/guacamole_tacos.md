## Guacamole Tacos

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 5 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 4 cups mashed ripe avocados
- Â½ 10-ounce can tomatoes & green chili
- 12 fully cooked taco shells
- garlic salt
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice 
- shredded lettuce
- taco sauce

## Instructions

1. **Mash up** ripe avocados until it has a paste texture

2. **Add** tomatoes & green chili, garlic salt, and lemon juice; mix well

2. **Heat** taco shells in oven at 200 degrees for 5 minutes

4. **Spoon** guacamole into shells

5. **Fill** shells with lettuce and top with taco sauce if desired
