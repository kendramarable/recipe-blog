#  Bean Tacos

### Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 5 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 can refried beans
- 12 tortillas
- shortening
- taco sauce
- shredded lettuce
- shredded cheese

## Instructions

1. **Heat** refried beans

2. **Fry** tortillas, one at a time, in 2 tablespoons hot shortening in a small skillet for a few seconds; drain

3. **Spoon** 2 tablespoons of refried beans on each tortilla; fold

4. **Fry** in small amount of hot shortening until crisp

5. **Fill** tacos with shredded lettuce and cheese

6. **Spoon** taco sauce over cheese


*source: grandma's recipe, originally from "Old El Paso Recipes for Tacos"*
