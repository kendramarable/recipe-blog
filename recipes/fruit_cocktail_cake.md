## Fruit Cocktail Cake

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 45 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients

### Cake: 
- 2 cups straight grade flour
- 1 ½ cups sugar
- ½ cup brown sugar
- ½ teaspoons baking soda
- ½ teaspoons salt
- ½ cups shortening
- 2 eggs
- 1 16 oz. can fruit cocktail

### Topping:
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 stick butter
- 1 12 oz. can evaporated milk
- 1 cup coconut flakes
- some vanilla


## Instructions: Cake

1. **Sift together** the flour, sugar, baking soda, and salt

2. **Mix** the sifted ingredients together with the shortening, eggs, and fruit cocktail

3. **Put** the mixture into a pan, then sprinkle the brown sugar on top

4. **Bake** in oven for 45 minutes at 350 degrees

## Instructions: Topping

1. **Boil** the sugar, butter, evaporated milk, and vanilla together for 3 minutes

2. **Pour** the mixture over the cake

3. **Sprinkle** the coconut flakes on top
