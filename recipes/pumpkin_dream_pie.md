# Pumpkin Dream Pie

#### Prep Time: 2 hours | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 tablespoon gelatin
- ¼ cup cold water
- 1 ¾ mashed cooked pumpkin
- ¾ cup milk
- 2 eggs
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
- 1 envelope Dream Whip
- ½ teaspoon vanilla
- 1 baked 9-inch pie shell


## Instructions

1. **Soften** gelatin in water

2. **Cook** pumpkin, milk, egg yolks, ½ cup sugar, salt, and spice over boiling water for 10 minutes, stirring constantly

3. **Stir in** gelatin

4. **Chill** until thick

5. **Prepare** Dream Whip as directed on package

6. **Beat** egg whites until stiff, adding remaining sugar gradually

7. **Fold** pumpkin, vanilla, 1 cup Dream Whip into egg whites

8. **Pour** into pie shell and let chill

9. **Top** with Dream Whip


###### *source: grandma's recipe, originally from Dream Whip*
