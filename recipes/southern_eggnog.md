# Southern Eggnog

#### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 0 minutes | Makes Several Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 package dessert mix
- 4 egg yolks
- 4 egg whites
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 1 ½ cups heavy cream
- nutmeg
- rum or brandy (optional)

## Instructions

1. **Empty** package of dessert mix into a bowl

2. **Add** egg yolks to dessert mix

3. **Beat** until thick and lemon colored

4. **Whip** cream and combine with the first mixture

5. **Fold in** beaten egg whites

6. **Chill** before serving

7. **Top** with nutmeg

8. **Optional**: to thin, add 1 ½ cups of milk, rum, or brandy
