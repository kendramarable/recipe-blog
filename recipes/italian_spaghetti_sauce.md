## Italian Spaghetti Sauce

#### Prep Time: 25 minutes | Cook Time: 120 minutes | Makes Several Servings

## Ingredients
 
- ½ cup sliced onions
- 2 tablespoons bacon fat
- 1 pound ground chuck
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- ½ green bell pepper
- 2 16-ounce cans tomatoes
- 1 2 ½-ounce can chopped mushrooms
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- 1 cup water
- 1 teaspoon sage
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- ½ teaspoon black pepper
- ¼ teaspoon thyme
- ¼ teaspoon oregano
- 2 8-ounce tomato sauce cans


## Instructions

1. **Rinse out** tomato cans and tomato sauce cans

2. **Saute** onions in bacon fat in large skillet until tender

3. **Add** meat, stirring until it loses its red color

4. **Drain off** grease and water out of meat and onions

5. **Put** in large pot with tomatoes and all other ingredients

6. **Simmer** uncovered for two hours, or until cooked down thick
