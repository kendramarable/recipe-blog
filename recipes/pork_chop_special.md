# Pork Chop Special

#### Prep Time: 25 minutes | Cook Time: 40 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 4 pork chops
- 1 tablespoon flour
- 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
- 2 ½ tablespoons parmesan cheese
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅛ teaspoon pepper
- 2 cups potatoes, thinly sliced
- 2 beef bouillon cubes
- ¼ cup + 2 tablespoons hot water
- 1 ½ teaspoons lemon juice

## Instructions

1. **Dredge** the pork chops in flour

2. **Brown** in hot oil in a medium skillet

3. **Combine** cheese, salt, and pepper; sprinkle 1 tablespoon of this mixture over pork chops

4. **Arrange** potatoes over meat

5. **Sprinkle** 1 tablespoon cheese mixture over potatoes

6. **Arrange** ⅓ cup chopped onion on top

7. **Dissolve** bouillon cubes in hot water; stir in lemon juice and pour over onion

8. **Sprinkle** remaining cheese on top

9. **Cover** and simmer for 40 minutes
