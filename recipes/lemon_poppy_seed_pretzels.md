# Lemon Poppy Seed Pretzels

### Prep Time: 60 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Makes 25 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 ¾ cups flour
- 1 tablespoon poppy seeds (plus more for sprinkling)
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- 6 ounces unsalted butter, room temperature
- 2 ½ cups confectioners sugar
- 1 ¼ teaspoons grated lemon zest
- ¼ teaspoon almond extract
- 1 egg
- 3 tablespoons milk
- yellow food coloring

## Instructions

1. **Combine** flour, 1 tablespoon of poppy seeds, and salt

2. **Beat** butter, 1 cup of confectioners sugar, and 1 teaspoon of lemon zest until creamy

3. **Beat in** almond extract and the egg

4. **Beat in** flour mixture

5. **Chill** dough

6. **Preheat** oven to 325 degrees

7. **Divide** dough into 25 pieces; roll each into a 10-inch length

8. **Shape** pieces into pretzels and put on ungreased baking sheets

9. **Bake** at 325 degrees until edges start to brown, about 15-20 minutes

10. **Let cool** on wire racks

11. **Combine** remaining 1 ½ cups confectioners sugar, ¼ teaspoon lemon zest, and milk

12. **Add** food coloring to tint the icing pale yellow

13. **Spoon** icing over cookies

14. **Let** excess icing drip back into the bowl

15. **Put** cookies on wire racks and sprinkle with poppy seeds

16. **Let dry** before serving
