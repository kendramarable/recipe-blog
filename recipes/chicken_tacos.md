#  Chicken Tacos

#### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- ½ cup chopped onion
- 2 tablespoons butter or margarine
- 2 cups cut-up cooked chicken
- taco sauce
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon garlic salt
- 12 taco shells
- shredded lettuce
- shredded cheese

## Instructions

1. **Cook** onion in butter or margarine in 10-inch skillet over medium heat until tender

2. **Add** chicken, salt, and garlic salt

3. **Cook** over low heat for 15 minutes

4. **Spoon** filling into taco shells

5. **Garnish** with shredded lettuce and cheese

6. **Spoon** taco sauce over cheese
