# Oatmeal Cookies

#### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Makes 30 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 1 cup white sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- 1 cup nuts
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 ½ cups flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- 3 cups oats

## Instructions

1. **Cream** butter, brown sugar, and white sugar

2. **Add** eggs, one at a time

3. **Add** vanilla, salt, flour, and baking soda

4. **Fold in** oats and nuts

5. **Drop** by teaspoons onto cookie sheets

6. **Bake** at 350 degrees until browned, about 10-15 minutes
