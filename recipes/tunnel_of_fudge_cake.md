# Tunnel of Fudge Cake

### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 70 minutes | Makes 12 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 cup butter
- 6 eggs
- 2 cups self-rising flour
- 1 Â½ cups sugar
- 1 package fudge buttercream frosting mix
- 2 cups chopped walnuts

## Instructions

1. **Cream** butter in large mixer bowl at high speed of electric mixer

2. **Add** eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition

3. **Gradually add** flour, then sugar, mixing until well-blended

4. **Stir in** frosting mix and walnuts; blend well

5. **Pour** batter into a greased bundt pan or 10-inch tube pan

6. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 65-70 minutes

7. **Cool** for 2 hours

8. **Remove** from pan

9. **Cool completely** before serving
