# Cheese Ball

### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 0 minutes | Makes Several Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 8-ounce packages cream cheese
- 1 8-ounce package sharp cheddar cheese
- 1 tablespoon worcestershire sauce
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice
- 1 tablespoon onion, chopped finely
- 1 tablespoon pimento, chopped
- 1 tablespoon green pepper, chopped
- cayenne pepper
- chopped nuts

## Instructions

1. **Blend** together the cheeses, worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, onion, pimento, and green pepper

2. **Add** a dash of cayenne pepper

3. **Roll** on waxed paper to create a ball

4. **Press** chopped nuts into roll
