## Dill Pickles

#### Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Makes Several Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 cups water
- 1 cup vinegar
- 5 teaspoons salt
- 2-3 teaspoons dill seed
- cucumbers
- jars to store

## Instructions

1. **Mix** water, vinegar, salt, and dill seed in a large pan

2. **Heat** jars

3. **Heat** large pan to boiling

4. **Add** cucumbers to pan, heat until the color changes

5. **Pack** cucumbers in hot jars; fill with hot liquid from pan

6. **Put** lids on jars; make sure lids are sealed tightly
