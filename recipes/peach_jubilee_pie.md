#  Peach Jubilee Pie

#### Prep Time: 25 minutes | Cook Time: 0 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 9-inch crust pastry
- 1 can (1 pound 5 ounces) peach pie filling
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- ½ teaspoon cinnamon
- ¼ teaspoon nutmeg
- 1 envelope whipped topping mix (2 to 2 ½ cups yield)
- ¼ cup sugar
- 1 cup (8 ounce carton) commercial sour cream
- maraschino cherries, if desired

## Instructions

1. **Combine** peach pie filling, sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg; set aside

2. **Prepare** whipped topping mix as directed on package EXCEPT omit 2 tablespoons of the milk; beat until stiff

3. **Add** ¼ cup sugar at low speed

4. **Gently fold in** the sour cream

5. **Spoon** about ½ of the peach mixture into a baked and cooled 9-inch pastry shell

6. **Spread** about ⅔ of the whipped topping mixture over the peaches, then gently spoon the remaining peaches over the whipped topping layer

7. **Swirl** a spoonful of the remaining whipped topping mixture on top of each serving; top with a maraschino cherry, if desired

8. **Chill** until firm, about 2 hours
