## Yeast Rolls

#### Prep Time: 150 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Makes Several Servings

## Ingredients
 
- ½ yeast cake or package
- 1 cup + ¼ cup lukewarm water
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- ¼ cup powdered milk
- 2 tablespoons melted shortening
- 3 to 4 cups sifted flour

## Instructions

1. **Dissolve** yeast in ¼ cup lukewarm water

2. **Sift together** sugar, salt, and powdered milk

3. **Add** 1 cup of water, gradually

4. **Stir** until well-dissolved, then add dissolved yeast and melted shortening

5. **Add** flour; mix well

6. **Knead** lightly on floured board

7. **Place** in a greased bowl; cover and allow to rise for about 1 ¼ hours

8. **Return** dough to board and shape into rolls

9. **Place** rolls in a greased pan and let rise for about 45 minutes

10. **Bake** in oven at 425 degrees for 12-15 minutes
