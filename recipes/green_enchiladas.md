## Green Enchiladas

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 10-ounce cans green enchilada sauce
- 12 tortillas
- 2 tablespoons shortening
- 2 medium onions, chopped
- Â½ pound shredded cheddar cheese

## Instructions: Flat

1. **Heat** enchilada sauce in small skillet until steaming

2. **Fry** tortillas, one at a time, in hot shortening in another small skillet for a few seconds

2. **Drain**, then dip tortillas in enchilada sauce

4. **Place** tortillas in large shallow baking pan

5. **Sprinkle** with onions and cheese

6. **Repeat**, making 4 stacks of 3 tortillas each

7. **Pour** remaining sauce over stacks

8. **Optional**: spoon sour cream between layers of tortillas and on top of stack

8. **Heat** in oven at 350 degrees for 10 minutes

## Instructions: Rolled

1. **Fry** tortillas, one at a time, in 2 tablespoons of hot shortening

2. **Drain**; fill each fortilla with chopped onion and grated cheese, and roll

3. **Lay** in a shallow casserole dish (9x14 inch)

4. **Cover** with enchilada sauce and top with grated cheese

5. **Heat** at 350 degrees until hot and cheese is melted
