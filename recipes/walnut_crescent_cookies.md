# Walnut Crescent Cookies

### Prep Time: 50 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Makes 24 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 cup margarine, softened
- ½ cup powdered sugar
- 2 teaspoons vanilla
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 1 ¾ cups flour
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- ½ cup granulated sugar

## Instructions

1. **Cream** butter, powdered sugar, vanilla, and salt in a large bowl until fluffy

2. **Stir in** flour and nuts until well-blended

3. **Cover and chill** for 30 minutes, or until firm enough to handle

4. **Break off** small pieces and roll on a lightly floured surface

5. **Roll** with hands into finger-thick strips

6. **Cut** into 2-inch length tapers and shape into crescents

7. **Bake** at 300 degrees for 18-20 minutes

8. **Roll** in granulated sugar
