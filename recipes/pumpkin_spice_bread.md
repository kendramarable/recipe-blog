## Pumpkin Spice Bread

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 55 minutes | Makes 16 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 3 ½ cups self-rising flour
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon cloves
- ½ teaspoon allspice
- ½ teaspoon nutmeg
- 2 ½ cups sugar
- ½ cup packed brown sugar
- 1 cup vegetable oil
- 4 eggs
- 1 16-ounce can pumpkin


## Instructions

1. **Preheat** oven to 350 degrees

2. **Grease and flour** two 9x5x3-inch loaf pans

3. **Mix** flour, cinnamon, cloves, allspice, and nutmeg

4. **Combine** sugars, oil, and eggs; beat well until blended

5. **Add** pumpkin, blend well

6. **Add** dry ingredients, blend well

7. **Put** batter into prepared pans

8. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 55 minutes

9. **Cool** in pans for 15 minutes
