## Egg Nog Cheesecake

#### Prep Time: 35 minutes | Cook Time: 0 minutes | Makes 12 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 packages chocolate laced Pirouette cookies
- ⅓ cup graham cracker crumbs
- 3 tablespoons margarine, melted
- 2 8-ounce packages cream cheese, softened
- 2 cups cold egg nog
- 1 ¾ cups cold milk
- 2 4-ounce packages instant French vanilla pudding
- 1 tablespoon rum
- ⅛ teaspoon nutmeg
- whipped cream

## Instructions

1. **Reserve** one of the Pirouette cookies

2. **Cut** a 1-inch piece off one end of the remaining cookies for the sides of the cake

3. **Crust** the 1-inch cookie pieces for crumbs

4. **Combine** cookie crumbs, graham cracker crumbs, and margarine

5. **Mix and press** onto a 9-inch springform pan

6. **Beat** cream cheese at low speed of electric mixer until smooth

7. **Gradually add** 1 cup of egg nog, blending until smooth

8. **Add** the remaining egg nog, milk, pudding mix, rum, and nutmeg

9. **Beat** until well-blended

10. **Pour** mixture into pan

11. **Chill** for 3 hours

12. **Press** cookies to sides of cake

13. **Garnish** with whipped cream
