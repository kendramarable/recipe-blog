## Ricotta Spinach Dip With Chips

#### Prep Time: 25 minutes | Cook Time: 0 minutes | Makes Several Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 bunch fresh spinach (approximately ¾ pound)
- 1 pound ricotta cheese
- 3 to 4 scallions, very finely minced (or 1 cup finely minced fresh chives)
- 5 to 6 healthy radishes, finely minced
- salt and freshly ground black pepper, to taste
- 1 to 2 tablespoons fresh dill, minced (optional)
- ½ cup finely minced water chestnuts (optional)
- optional garnishes: parsley, olives, cherry tomatoes


## Instructions

1. **Stem** the spinach and clean it thoroughly in several changes of cold water

2. **Dry** completely using a salad spinner followed by paper towels, and mince fine

3. **Place** the ricotta in a medium-large bowl

4. **Use** a wire whisk or a wooden spoon to beat ricotta until smooth

5. **Stir** in the spinach and all other ingredients, except garnishes, including (or not) the optional dill and minced water chestnuts

6. **Cover** tightly, and chill until very cold

7. **Serve** with chips, raw vegetables, or crackers
