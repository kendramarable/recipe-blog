## French Onion Soup

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 40 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 stick butter
- 2 large onions, sliced
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 cup red wine
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 2 32-ounce boxes beef stock
- fresh thyme
- baguette croutons
- gruyère cheese

## Instructions

1. **Melt** butter in a pot over medium heat

2. **Add** garlic and onions; cook down and caramelize onions

3. **Add** red wine, cooking down again

4. **Pull out** stems of thyme; add to mixture

5. **Dust** onions with flour; stir

6. **Add** beef stock to mixture; stir

7. **Put** mixture in an oven-safe container

8. **Put** gruyère cheese and baguette croutons on top of soup

9. **Broil** soup in oven for 2-5 minutes
