# Banana Buttermilk Pound Cake

### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 50 minutes | Makes 12 Servings

## Ingredients: Cake
 
- 2 ½ cups flour
- ½ cup self-rising flour
- ½ teaspoon baking powder
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup vegetable oil
- 2 ½ cups sugar
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup buttermilk
- ½ teaspoon baking soda, added to buttermilk
- 3 small bananas, sliced

## Ingredients: Icing

- ½ banana, mashed
- 2 cups powdered sugar
- 1 tablespoon lemon juice


## Instructions

1. **Sift together** both flours, baking powder, and salt; set aside

2. **Cream** vegetable oil and sugar until fluffy

3. **Add** eggs and buttermilk alternatively to flour mixture

4. **Beat** for 2 minutes at a high speed

5. **Fold in** sliced bananas

6. **Pour** batter into a well-greased and floured bundt pan

7. **Bake** at 325 degrees for 50 minutes

8. **Mix together** ingredients for the icing; drizzle on top of cake
