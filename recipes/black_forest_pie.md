#  Black Forest Pie

### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 0 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 3 ½ cups (8 ounces) Cool Whip
- 1 cup milk
- 1 package instant chocolate pudding
- 1 cup cherry pie filling
- 1 8- or 9-inch pie crust

## Instructions

1. **Spread** 1 cup of the Cool Whip into pie crust

2. **Blend** milk and pudding at low speed for 1 minute

3. **Add** 1 ½ cups of Cool Whip

4. **Put** mixture in crust; spread remaining Cool Whip on top

5. **Indent** in center and put pie filling on top

6. **Chill** for 2 hours before serving
