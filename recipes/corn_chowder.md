## Corn Chowder

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 35 minutes | Makes 6 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 4 slices bacon, diced
- 1 cup onion
- 1 medium potato, diced
- ¾ cup celery, sliced
- 1 can chicken broth
- ½ teaspoon thyme
- 1 cup half and half
- 1 can cream-style corn
- 1 tablespoon minced parsley
- dash of pepper

## Instructions

1. **Fry** bacon until crisp in a heavy saucepan

2. **Add** onion, potato, and celery; quickly saute

3. **Stir in** chicken broth, thyme, and pepper

4. **Bring** to a boil

5. **Reduce** heat

6. **Cover and simmer** for 15-20 minutes

7. **Add** half and half, corn, and parsley

8. **Mix** well

9. **Heat** through
