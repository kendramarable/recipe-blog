# Bacon & Cheese Mushrooms

### Prep Time: 35 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 pound (about 8) large domestic mushrooms
- 3 ounces bacon, chopped
- 2 garlic cloves, minced
- 2 tablespoons bread crumbs
- Â¼ cup mozzarella cheese, grated
- 2 tablespoons parmesan cheese, grated
- butter for coating



## Instructions

1. **Preheat** oven to 350 degrees

2. **Coat** a baking sheet with butter

3. **Wipe** mushrooms clean and gently remove stems

4. **Place** caps hollow-side up on buttered tray

5. **Chop** stems and set aside

6. **Fry** bacon in small skillet over medium-low heat

7. **Transfer** bacon bits to paper towels to drain, using a slotted spoon

8. **Bring** skillet with bacon fat to high heat and saute garlic and chopped stems for about a minute

9. **Transfer** to medium bowl and add bacon, bread crumbs, and cheeses

10. **Mix** with a fork

11. **Stuff** a heaping spoonful of a mixture into each mushroom cap

12. **Place** in oven and bake until top is browned, about 15 minutes

13. **Serve** hot
