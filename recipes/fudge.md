## Fudge

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Makes 24 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- ¾ cup evaporated milk
- 2 ¼ cups sugar
- 2 6-ounce packages chocolate chips
- ⅓ cup light corn syrup
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 1 cup chopped nuts

## Instructions

1. **Combine** milk and sugar in heavy saucepan

2. **Cook** over medium heat, stirring constantly, until mixture boils

3. **Turn** heat to low

4. **Cook** for 10 minutes, stirring to prevent scorching

5. **Remove** from heat

6. **Add** chocolate chips, butter, and vanilla

7. **Stir** until melted

8. **Add** nuts

9. **Spread** onto a buttered dish

10. **Chill** before serving
