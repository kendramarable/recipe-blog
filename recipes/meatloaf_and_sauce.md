# Meatloaf and Sauce

### Prep Time: 25 minutes | Cook Time: 60 minutes | Makes 5 Servings

## Ingredients: Meatloaf
 
- 1 ½ pounds ground beef
- 1 cup dry bread crumbs
- 1 small onion, chopped
- 1 egg
- 1 ½ teaspoons salt
- ¼ teaspoon pepper
- 4 tablespoons ketchup
- ½ cup milk

## Ingredients: Sauce

- 4 tablespoons vinegar
- 4 tablespoons brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons worcestershire sauce
- ½ cup ketchup
- ¼ cup chopped onion


## Instructions

1. **Mix** meatloaf ingredients together and shape into a loaf

2. **Mix** sauce ingredients together and bring to a boil

3. **Simmer** sauce for 10 minutes

4. **Pour** sauce over meatloaf

5. **Bake** at 325 degrees for 1 hour
