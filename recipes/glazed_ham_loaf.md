# Glazed Ham Loaf

#### Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 80 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 pound lean ham, ground up
- 1 pound lean fresh pork
- ¾ cup cracker crumbs
- ¼ cup chopped onion
- 2 beaten eggs
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup chopped parsley
- ¼ cup apple cider vinegar
- ¾ tablespoon dry mustard
- ½ cup brown sugar

## Instructions

1. **Mix** the ham, pork, cracker crumbs, onion, eggs, salt, and parsley

2. **Shape** mixture into a loaf, then put into a large loaf pan

3. **Cook** at 350 degrees for one hour

4. **Mix** the apple cider vinegar, dry mustard, and brown sugar together; boil for 1 minute to create the glaze

6. **Baste** the ham loaf with the glaze

7. **Cook** at 350 degrees for 15-20 minutes longer
