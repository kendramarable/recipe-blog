## Chocolate Sauce

#### Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Makes Several Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 can (1 ⅓ cups) sweetened condensed milk
- 2 squares (2 ounces) unsweetened chocolate
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- ½ to 1 cup hot water
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

## Instructions

1. **Put** condensed milk, chocolate, and salt in top of double boiler

2. **Cook** over rapidly boiling water, stirring often, until thick (about 10 minutes)

3. **Remove** from heat

4. **Slowly stir** in water, amount depending on consistency desired

5. **Let cool**, then stir in vanilla

6. **Serve** with ice cream, cake, or pudding


###### *source: grandma's recipe, originally from Borden*
