# Cinnamon Ring

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes | Makes 24 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 3 cans 8-count biscuits
- 1 cup sugar
- Â¼ cup brown sugar
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 stick butter


## Instructions

1. **Mix** sugar, brown sugar, and cinnamon

2. **Cut** biscuits into halves

3. **Dip** biscuits in mixture and place in a well-greased bundt pan

4. **Pour** remaining mixture over the biscuits

5. **Melt** butter and pour over mixture

6. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 30 minutes
