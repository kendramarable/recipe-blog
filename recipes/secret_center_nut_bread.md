## Secret Center Nut Bread

#### Prep Time: 30 minutes | Cook Time: 85 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients: Filling
 
- 2 3-ounce packages cream cheese, softened
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 tablespoon grated orange peel
- ¾ cup sugar

## Ingredients: Bread

- ⅔ cup honey
- ⅔ cup milk
- 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
- ⅓ cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon soda
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ cup solid hydrogenated vegetable shortening
- 1 egg
- 1 cup chopped nuts

## Instructions: Filling

1. **Combine** all ingredients in a small mixer bowl

2. **Blend** at low speed, just until well-mixed

3. **Set** mixture aside

## Instructions: Bread

1. **Combine** honey and milk in large mixer bowl

2. **Add** flour, sugar, soda, salt, shortening, and egg

3. **Blend** at low speed just until smooth, about 1 minute, scraping sides of bowl frequently

4. **Stir** in nuts

5. **Spread** half the batter in a greased and floured 9x5x3 inch pan

6. **Pour** filling over batter

7. **Carefully spoon** remaining batter over filling; gently spread

8. **Bake** at 325 degrees for 1 hour 15 minutes to 1 hour 25 minutes

9. **Let cool** for 15 minutes

10. **Remove** from pan; let cool on wire rack

11. **Wrap tightly** in aluminum foil and store in refrigerator
