# Hamburger Corn Casserole

### Prep Time: 35 minutes | Cook Time: 45 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 ½ pounds ground beef
- 3 cups noodles, cooked
- 1 cup onion, chopped
- 1 can whole kernel corn
- 1 can cream of chicken soup
- 1 can cream of mushroom soup
- ¼ cup pimentos
- bread crumbs, buttered

## Instructions

1. **Brown** the ground beef

2. **Add** the chopped onion

3. **Cook** until tender; drain

4. **Add** can of whole kernel corn

5. **Add** can of cream of mushroom soup

6. **Add** pimentos; stir

7. **Add** the cooked noodles; stir

8. **Pour** into a casserole dish

9. **Top** with buttered bread crumbs

10. **Bake** at 350 degrees until done, about 35-45 minutes
