#  Self-Frosted Fudge Cake

#### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 45 minutes | Makes 12 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 tablespoons plus ½ cup butter, softened
- 1 ¼ cups all-purpose flour
- ¾ cup sugar
- 2 ½ teaspoons baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ⅔ cup milk
- 2 1-ounce envelopes pre-melted unsweetened chocolate
- 2 eggs
- ½ cup salted pecan halves
- 1 package chocolate fudge sauce mix
- ¾ cup plus 2 tablespoons warm water

## Instructions

1. **Melt** 2 tablespoons of butter in a 9- or 10-inch ovenproof skillet; set aside

2. **Measure** all ingredients except pecans, dry sauce mix, and water into a large mixer bowl

3. **Blend well** at low speed; beat 2 minutes at medium speed

4. **Pour** mixture into skillet

5. **Sprinkle** mixture with pecans

6. **Combine** water and dry sauce mix; spoon over batter

7. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 40 to 45 minutes

8. **Cool** for 1 to 2 minutes before inverting onto a serving plate

9. **Serve** warm with ice cream or whipped cream

10. **Note**: can substitute the fudge sauce mix with ¾ cup chocolate syrup; decrease water to ½ cup
