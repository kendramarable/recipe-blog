# Baked Ziti

#### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Makes 6 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 package ziti
- spaghetti sauce
- 8 ounces ricotta cheese
- 1 Â½ cups mozzarella cheese
- parmesan cheese


## Instructions

1. **Boil** ziti for a few minutes, making sure not to overcook

2. **Layer** spaghetti sauce in a square baking pan

3. **Mix** cooked ziti with the ricotta cheese and some of the mozzarella cheese

4. **Layer** over the sauce

5. **Top** with more mozzarella cheese, then some parmesan cheese

6. **Bake** at 350 degrees until bubbly and browned, about 20 minutes
