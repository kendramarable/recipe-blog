## Beef Stir Fry With Mushrooms

#### Prep Time: 90 minutes | Cook Time: 25 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 pound boneless beef
- ¼ cup soy sauce
- ⅓ cup water
- 2 tablespoons dry red wine
- 1 tablespoon molasses or honey
- 4 teaspoons corn starch
- 2 tablespoons cooking oil
- 1 cup green onions, cut into 2-inch strips
- 3 cups fresh mushrooms, sliced
- hot cooked rice

## Instructions

1. **Partially freeze** beef

2. **Cut** beef into thinly sliced strips

3. **Combine** soy sauce, water, wine, molasses/honey, and corn starch

4. **Place** beef in a medium bowl

5. **Pour** marinade over beef

6. **Let stand** at room temperature for 30 minutes

7. **Drain** and reserve marinade

8. **Preheat** a wok

9. **Add** oil to wok

10. **Add** green onions and mushrooms to wok; remove

11. **Add** half of meat to wok

12. **Stir fry** meat for 2 to 3 minutes until brown; remove

13. **Stir fry** remaining meat

14. **Return** all beef to wok; add marinade

15. **Cook and stir** until bubbly

16. **Return** vegetables to wok

17. **Cover and cook** for 2 minutes

18. **Serve** over rice
