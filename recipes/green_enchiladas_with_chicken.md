## Green Enchiladas with Chicken

#### Prep Time: 35 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 12 tortillas
- 1 pint sour cream
- ¾ pound cheddar cheese, grated
- 2 tablespoons shortening
- 3-4 chicken breasts
- 2 10-ounce cans green chili enchilada sauce
- 1 7 ½-ounce can taco sauce
- 1 4-ounce can chopped green chili
- 2 medium onions, chopped

## Instructions

1. **Boil** chicken breasts

2. **Bone and dice** chicken once cooked

3. **Heat** green chili sauce and taco sauce in small skillet until steaming

4. **Fry** tortillas, one at a time, in 2 tablespoons of hot shortening in another small skillet for a few seconds

5. **Drain**, then dip tortillas in green chili sauce

6. **Place** tortillas in a large shallow baking pan

7. **Sprinkle** tortillas with cheese, onion, and diced chicken

8. **Cover** with a thin coat of green chili sauce and 1-2 teaspoons of sour cream

9. **Repeat** this process, making 4 stacks of 3 tortillas each

10. **Place** platter with 4 stacks of enchiladas in oven at 350 degrees for 30 minutes
