# Lemon Cloud Pie

### Prep Time: 30 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 cup self-rising flour
- ⅓ cup shortening
- 1 egg
- 1 package lemon pudding and pie filling mix
- 2 eggs, separated
- 1 3-ounce package cream cheese, room temperature
- ¼ cup sugar
- 1 8- or 9-inch pie pan


## Instructions

1. **Measure** flour and shortening into small mixer bowl

2. **Cut** shortening into flour until particles are the size of small peas, using lowest speed of mixer

3. **Add** the egg and mix until a dough forms

4. **Roll out** between 2 sheets of waxed paper to a circle 1 ½ inches larger than an inverted 8- or 9-inch pie pan

5. **Fit** waxed paper loosely into pie pan

6. **Trim** pastry from edge of shell and place extra pieces in a small baking pan

7. **Fold** edge to form a rim; flute

8. **Prick** generously with a fork

9. **Bake** shell and pastry pieces at 400 degrees for 12 to 15 minutes; cool

10. **Prepare** pie filling according to package directions, using 2 egg yolks

11. **Stir** in cream cheese; beat well

12. **Combine** egg whites and sugar; beat until stiff peaks form

13. **Fold** into lemon mixture

14. **Spoon** into baked pastry shell

15. **Chill** for at least 2 hours

16. **Sprinkle** crumbled pastry around edge of pie before serving
