# Ginger Pear Cake

#### Prep Time: 30 minutes | Cook Time: 40 minutes | Makes 12 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 tablespoons butter or margarine
- ¼ cup brown sugar
- ¼ cup dark corn syrup
- 1 1-pint can pear halves, drained
- ½ cup walnut halves
- 1 14 ½-ounce package gingerbread mix


## Instructions

1. **Melt** butter or margarine in a 9x13 inch pan

2. **Stir in** brown sugar and corn syrup; blend well

3. **Cut** pear halves in half, forming quarters

4. **Place** a walnut half in the cavity of each quarter

5. **Arrange** in pan in a spiral fashion

6. **Place** nuts in center

7. **Prepare** cake mix according to package

8. **Pour** cake mix over pears

9. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 40 minutes
