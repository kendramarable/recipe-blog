## Mushroom Pork Tenderloin

#### Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- ¼ cup mayonnaise
- 1 1-ounce packet onion soup mix
- 3 cloves fresh garlic
- 1 ½ pounds pork tenderloin
- 1 8-ounce package sliced baby portabella mushrooms
- 1 tablespoon quick-mixing flour
- ½ cup milk

## Instructions

1. **Combine** the mayonnaise and dry soup mix into a large bowl

2. **Crush** garlic, using a garlic press, into bowl

3. **Mix** well

4. **Slice** tenderloin diagonally into ½-inch thick slices

5. **Preheat** large saute pan on medium high for 2-3 minutes

6. **Stir** pork slices and rinsed mushrooms into soup mixture

7. **Place** tenderloin, using tongs, into pan

8. **Add** soup mixture

9. **Cook** for 4-6 minutes, turning occasionally, until browned

10. **Stir in** flour, and cook for 1-2 more minutes

11. **Stir in** milk, then reduce heat to low

12. **Cook** for 4-6 minutes until sauce thickens and internal temperature of pork slices reaches 150 degrees
