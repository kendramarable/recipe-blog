## Cornbread

#### Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 25 minutes | Makes 9 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup white corn meal
- 1 cup enriched flour
- 3 tablespoons baking powder
- 1 tablespoon sugar
- Â½ teaspoon salt
- 3 tablespoons melted shortening
- 1 cup milk

## Instructions

1. **Mix** all dry ingredients

2. **Add** eggs and milk, stirring until well-blended

3. **Stir** in melted shortening

4. **Pour** into greased pan

5. **Bake** at 350 degrees until golden brown, about 25 minutes
