## Simple Bread

#### Prep Time: 90 minutes | Cook Time: 40 minutes | Makes 20 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 ½ cups warm water
- 2 packages active dry yeast
- ½ cup instant non-fat dry milk
- 2 tablespoons sugar
- 1 tablespoon salt
- ⅓ cup cooking oil or shortening
- 7 ½ cups all-purpose flour

## Instructions

1. **Measure** water into large mixer bowl

2. **Sprinkle** yeast over water

3. **Add** dry milk, sugar, salt, oil, and 3 ¼ cups flour to make a thick batter

4. **Blend** well; beat 3 minutes at medium speed

5. **Gradually add** remaining flower by hand to form a very stiff dough

6. **Toss** dough on floured surface until no longer sticky

7. **Knead** for 1 to 2 minutes, until smooth

8. **Divide** dough in half

9. **Shape** dough using a rolling pin into a 12x6 inch rectangle

10. **Roll up** tightly, starting with 6-inch side

11. **Seal** edges and ends

12. **Place**, seam-side down, in well-greased 8 ½ x 4 ½ inch pans

13. **Cover** and let rise in a warm place until doubled in size, about 1 hour

14. **Bake** at 375 degrees for 35 to 40 minutes

15. **Remove** from pans immediately; brush with butter

16. **Let cool** on wire rack
