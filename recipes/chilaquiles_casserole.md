# Chilaquiles Casserole

#### Prep Time: 40 minutes | Cook Time: 45 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 pounds ground meat
- 2 medium onions, chopped
- 12 tortillas
- 2 10-ounce cans enchilada sauce
- Â½ teaspoon cumin
- 1 pound cheddar cheese, grated
- garlic salt
- pepper
- shortening


## Instructions

1. **Brown** meat, onion, and seasonings in small amount of shortening

2. **Add** enchilada sauce and cook for 20-30 minutes

3. **Arrange** alternate layers of cheese, meat mixture, and tortillas in deep baking dish

4. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 30 to 45 minutes
