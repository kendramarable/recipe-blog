## White Lily "Light" Biscuits

#### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Makes 12 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 cups White Lily self-rising flour
- ¼ cup shortening
- ¾ cup buttermilk


## Instructions

1. **Preheat** oven to 500 degrees

2. **Measure** flour into a bowl

3. **Put in** shortening until mixture is like coarse crumbs

4. **Blend in** just enough milk with fork until dough leaves sides of bowl

5. **Knead** gently on lightly floured surface for 10 to 12 strokes

6. **Roll** dough about ½ inch thick; cut without twisting cutter

7. **Bake** on ungreased baking sheet at 500 degrees for 10 minutes
