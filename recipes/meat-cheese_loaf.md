## Meat-Cheese Loaf

#### Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes | Makes 6 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 pound ground chuck
- 1 chopped onion
- 1 can undiluted cheddar cheese soup
- 1 loaf French bread
- salt to taste

## Instructions

1. **Brown** beef and onion; drain

2. **Add** salt and soup

3. **Cut** French bread in half lengthwise

4. **Pull out** center of bread and mix chunks with meat mixture

5. **Pile** mixture into bottom half of bread

6. **Put** top half on and wrap in foil

7. **Bake** in oven at 350 degrees for 30 minutes

8. **Slice** and serve
