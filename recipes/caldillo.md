# Caldillo

#### Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 110 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 pounds round steak
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- 1 garlic, minced
- 1 4-ounce can whole green chili
- ½ cup canned tomatoes
- 1 10-ounce can tomatoes & green chili
- ½ teaspoon cumin
- 1 teaspoon shortening
- 1 tablespoon flour



## Instructions

1. **Brown** meat, cut into cubes, in hot shortening

2. **Add** flour, onion, garlic, tomatoes, tomatoes & green chili, and cumin

3. **Add** water to desired consistency (to be eaten as soup or stew), and simmer until tender (about 1 ½ hours)

4. **Add** the green chili, cut in large pieces, to the stew, and let simmer another 20 minutes
