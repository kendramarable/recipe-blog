## Date and Nut Roll

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 0 minutes | Makes 10 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 cups (½ pound) vanilla wafer crumbs
- 1 cup chopped dates
- ½ cup chopped nut meats
- ½ cup sweetened condensed milk
- 2 teaspoons lemon juice

## Instructions

1. **Combine** water crumbs, dates, and nut meats

2. **Blend** condensed milk and lemon juice

3. **Add** blended mixture to crumb mixture; knead well

4. **Form** into roll 3 inches in diameter; cover with waxed paper

5. **Chill** in refrigerator for at least 12 hours

6. **Cut** in slices and garnish with whipped cream or hard sauce, if desired


###### *source: grandma's recipe, originally from Borden*
