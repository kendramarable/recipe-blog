#  Peanut Butter Cookies

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Makes 24 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 ½ cups flour
- ½ teaspoon baking soda
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ½ cup margarine
- ½ cups butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 cup brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup peanut butter
- 2 teaspoons vanilla

## Instructions

1. **Sift** all the dry ingredients together 3 times

2. **Cream** margarine and butter

3. **Add** brown sugar and white sugar; cream until smooth

4. **Beat** in eggs

5. **Add** peanut butter, vanilla, and dry ingredients mixture

6. **Knead** to form a soft dough

7. **Form** small round balls

8. **Lay** on ungreased cookie sheet

9. **Press down** with a fork and make a cross pattern on each cookie

10. **Bake** at 375 degrees for 12-15 minutes
