## Butter Mint Pats

#### Prep Time: 25 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Makes 36 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 cup butter, softened
- 1 cup butter mints, crushed
- 2 cups all-purpose flour
- 1 tablespoon sugar

## Instructions

1. **Cream** butter at medium speed in a large mixer bowl, until light

2. **Add** crushed mints and flour

3. **Blend well** at low speed

4. **Chill** dough for easier handling, if necessary

5. **Roll out** or pat dough on waxed paper into a 9-inch square (floured fingers help)

6. **Sprinkle** dough with sugar

7. **Press** a design on the surface of the dough using a floured thimble or small cookie cutter

8. **Cut** dough into 1 Â½ inch squares

9. **Place** squares on ungreased cookie sheets

10. **Bake** at 300 degrees for 18 to 20 minutes, until pale golden brown

11. **Note**: for chocolate butter-mint pats, add 1 to 2 tablespoons cocoa with the flour
