## Bread Pudding

#### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 60 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 can (1 ⅓ cups) sweetened condensed milk
- 3 cups hot water
- 2 cups finely diced bread or soft bread crumbs
- 3 eggs, slightly beaten
- 1 tablespoon melted butter
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract or grated lemon rind

## Instructions

1. **Combine** condensed milk and hot water

2. **Pour** over bread; let stand until lukewarm

3. **Stir** in eggs, butter, salt, and vanilla or lemon rind

4. **Pour** into greased 1 ½ quart casserole or baking dish

5. **Set** in shallow pan of hot water

6. **Bake** in oven at 350 degrees for about 1 hour, until a knife blade inserted near the center comes out clean

7. **Serve** hot or cold with ice cream, fruit, or preserves


###### *source: grandma's recipe, originally from Borden*
