# Pumpkin Pie

### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 50 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- ¾ cup brown sugar
- 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 ¼ teaspoon pumpkin pie spice
- 1 ½ cups canned pumpkin
- 1 ½ cups evaporated milk (PET)
- 1 egg, slightly beaten
- 1 9-inch unbaked pastry crust


## Instructions

1. **Mix** brown sugar, flour, salt, and pumpkin pie spice in a 3-quart bowl

2. **Stir** canned pumpkin, evaporated milk, and egg into the bowl until smooth

3. **Pour** mixture into pastry crust

4. **Bake** at 375 degrees for about 50 minutes, or until firm

## Note

- In place of pumpkin pie spice, you can use 1 teaspoon cinnamon, ½ teaspoon nutmeg, ¼ teaspoon, ginger, and ¼ teaspoon cloves


*source: grandma's recipe, originally from PET Evaporated Milk*
