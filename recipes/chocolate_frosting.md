# Chocolate Frosting

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 0 minutes | Makes Several Servings

## Ingredients
 
- ¼ cup Crisco shortening, melted
- ½ cup cocoa
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ⅓ cup milk
- 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla
- 3 ½ cups confectioners sugar, sift if lumpy

## Instructions

1. **Melt** the shortening in a saucepan

2. **Remove** from heat after melting; add cocoa and salt

3. **Stir in** milk and vanilla

4. **Put** the sugar in a bowl

5. **Add** the chocolate mixture to the sugar

6. **Mix** at medium speed until smooth and creamy
