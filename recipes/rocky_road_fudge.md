# Rocky Road Fudge

#### Prep Time: 30 minutes | Cook Time: 0 minutes | Makes 20 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 5 to 7 large marshmallows, cut in quarters (or ½ cup miniature marshmallows)
- 6 squares semi-sweet chocolate
- ½ cup butter or margarine
- 2 cups sugar
- 10 large marshmallows (or 1 cup miniature marshmallows)
- ⅔ cup (1 5 ⅓-ounce can) evaporated milk
- 1 cup chopped walnuts
- 1 teaspoon vanilla


## Instructions

1. **Freeze** quartered marshmallows until firm

2. **Place** chocolate and butter in large bowl

3. **Combine** sugar, whole marshmallows, and evaporated milk in heavy saucepan

4. **Bring** to a boil over medium heat; boil for 5 minutes, stirring constantly

5. **Pour** over chocolate and butter; blend well until mixture begins to thicken

6. **Stir** in walnuts, vanilla, and frozen marshmallows

7. **Pour** into lightly-greased 8-inch square pan

8. **Chill** until firm

9. **Cut** into squares

10. **Store** covered in a cool place
