## Lasagna

#### Prep Time: 30 minutes | Cook Time: 40 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 package mozzarella cheese, grated
- ½ pound cottage cheese
- parmesan cheese
- ½ pound cheddar cheese, grated
- 1 box wide lasagna noodles
- spaghetti sauce


## Instructions

1. **Cook** noodles, rinse, and drain

2. **Butter** a large baking dish or pan

3. **Spread** a layer of noodles in the pan, then part of the cheddar cheese, spaghetti sauce, then a layer of cottage cheese

4. **Add** another layer of noodles

5. **Add** another layer of mozzarella cheese

6. **Add** more spaghetti sauce, then the rest of the cheddar cheese

7. **Sprinkle** over the top with parmesan cheese

8. **Bake** in oven at 400 degrees for 30-40 minutes, or until bubbly and brown
