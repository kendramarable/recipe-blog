#  Cheese Straws

#### Prep Time: 75 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Makes Several Servings

## Ingredients
 
- ¾ cup butter
- 1 ½ sharp cheese, grated
- ¼ cup parmesan cheese
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- ⅛ teaspoon red pepper
- 1 ½ cup flour

## Instructions

1. **Cream** butter and cheese together

2. **Sift** dry ingredients together and work thoroughly into butter-cheddar mix

3. **Chill** for 1 hour

4. **Roll out** onto board into thin, oblong shape

5. **Cut** into thin strips about ½ inch wide and 5 inches long

6. **Place** onto a pan (not too close together)

7. **Place** into cold oven, turn oven to 300 degrees, and bake for 10-15 minutes
