# Honey Candy Bites

### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Makes 42 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- ½ cup butter
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 1 cup self-rising flour
- ¾ cup honey
- 1 cup grated coconut
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 cups Rice Krispies cereal or corn flakes, slightly crushed


## Instructions

1. **Combine** all ingredients except for vanilla extract and cereal in saucepan

2. **Cook** over medium heat, stirring constantly, until dough leaves sides of pan and forms a ball

3. **Remove** from heat, let cool

4. **Add** vanilla extract and cereal

5. **Shape** into 1 inch balls; roll in additional coconut

6. **Store** in refrigerator
