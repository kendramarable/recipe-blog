# Honey and Oat Granola Pie

### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 45 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 stick butter
- ½ cup brown sugar
- ¾ cup corn syrup
- 3 eggs
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- ⅛ teaspoon salt
- 2 packets oats and honey granola bars, crushed
- ½ cup oatmeal
- ½ cup walnuts
- ½ cup chocolate chips
- 1 8- or 9-inch pie crust

## Instructions

1. **Mix** butter and sugar together in a large bowl

2. **Add in** the rest of the ingredients (except the pie crust); mix well

3. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 45 minutes
