# Stuffed Crab

#### Prep Time: 60 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes | Makes 5 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 onion
- 1 bell pepper
- 1 stick of butter
- Â¾ sleeve of crackers, crumbled
- 1 egg
- black pepper or hot sauce
- large boiled crabs, peeled

## Instructions

1. **Cook** butter, bell pepper, and onion in a skillet until done

2. **Add** the crumbled crackers

3. **Add** the egg and either black pepper or hot sauce

4. **Stir** while simmering in skillet

5. **Add** crab meat and mix together

6. **Put** mixture into crab shells

7. **Bake** at 350 degrees until golden brown, about 30 minutes
