## Ice Cream

#### Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Makes Several Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 cups sugar
- ¼ cup corn starch
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- 3 cups milk
- 4 eggs, beaten
- 2 tablespoons vanilla
- 4 cups half and half
- 1 cup whipping cream

## Instructions

1. **Mix** sugar, corn starch, and salt in top of double boiler

2. **Blend in** milk gradually

3. **Cook** over boiling water for about 12-15 minutes

4. **Stir in** a small amount of corn starch mixture into the beaten eggs

5. **Stir** the eggs into the mixture

6. **Cook** for 4-5 minutes

7. **Chill** thoroughly

8. **Stir in** vanilla and chilled cream
