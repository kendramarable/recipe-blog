# Million Dollar Pie

#### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 0 minutes | Makes 16 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 graham cracker pie crusts
- 1 large or 2 small packages of Dream Whip
- 1 cup milk
- 1 can sweetened condensed milk
- 1 cup coconut
- 1 cup finely chopped pecans
- ½ cup red cherries, diced
- 1 large can crushed pineapple, drained
- ½ cup lemon juice

## Instructions

1. **Whip** the Dream Whip and milk

2. **Add** the sweetened condensed milk; whip again

3. **Add** the coconut, pecans, cherries, canned pineapple, and lemon juice

4. **Mix** together and pour into crusts

5. **Chill** before serving
