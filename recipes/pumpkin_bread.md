# Pumpkin Bread

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 60 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- ⅓ cup vegetable oil
- 1 cup canned pumpkin
- 3 eggs
- 1 ¼ cups sugar
- 2 ⅓ cups Bisquick
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon
- ½ cup nuts

## Instructions

1. **Preheat** oven to 350 degrees

2. **Grease** a loaf pan

3. **Mix** all ingredients except for the nuts

4. **Add** nuts

5. **Place** in loaf pan

6. **Bake** at 350 degrees for approximately 1 hour
