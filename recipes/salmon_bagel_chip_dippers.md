# Salmon Bagel Chip Dippers

#### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 3 minutes | Makes 12 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 14 ¾-ounce can or 2 7 ½-ounce cans Alaska canned salmon, drained and flaked
- 1 8-ounce container light cream cheese with chives or onion
- 2 tablespoons milk
- ½ teaspoon dried dill
- 1 6-ounce package garlic or plain bagel chips
- 1 4-ounce cup shredded light or regular mozzarella or monterey jack cheese
- 1 cup chopped ripe tomato
- ½ cup sliced green onions

## Instructions

1. **Combine** cream cheese, milk, and dill in a small bowl

2. **Stir** ½ cup salmon chunks into cream cheese mixture

3. **Arrange** bagel chips in a single layer on two 12-inch microwave-safe dinner or serving plates

4. **Randomly drop** cream cheese mixture by teaspoonfuls over chips

5. **Top** with remaining salmon and shredded cheese

6. **Cook**, one plate at a time, in microwave oven 2-3 minutes, or until cheese melts

7. **Top** with tomato and green onions

8. **Serve** warm or at room temperature
