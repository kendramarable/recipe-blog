## Orange Kiss-Me Cake

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 45 minutes | Makes 12 Servings

## Ingredients: Cake
 
- 1 6-ounce can (¾ cup) frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed
- 2 cups self-rising flour
- 1 cup sugar
- ¼ teaspoon soda
- ½ cup shortening
- ½ cup milk
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup raisins
- ⅓ cup chopped walnuts

## Ingredients: Topping

- ⅓ cup sugar
- ¼ cup chopped walnuts
- 1 teaspoon cinnamon


## Instructions

1. **Grease and flour** the bottom of a 13x9 inch pan

2. **Combine** ½ cup orange juice concentrate with remaining ingredients in a large mixer bowl

3. **Blend** at lowest speed for 30 seconds

4. **Beat** 3 minutes at medium speed

5. **Pour** mixture into pan

6. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 40-45 minutes until cake springs back when touched lightly in center

7. **Drizzle** remaining orange juice concentrate over warm cake

8. **Combine** ingredients for topping in a small bowl and sprinkle over cake
