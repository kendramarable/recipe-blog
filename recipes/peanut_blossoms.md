## Peanut Blossoms

#### Prep Time: 30 minutes | Cook Time: 12 minutes | Makes 48 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 ¾ cup self-rising flour
- ½ cup sugar
- ½ cup brown sugar, firmly packed
- ½ cup shortening
- ½ cup peanut butter
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons milk
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- 48 milk chocolate candy kisses


## Instructions

1. **Combine** all ingredients except for the candy in a large mixer bowl

2. **Mix** on lowest speed until dough forms

3. **Shape** dough into balls using a rounded teaspoonful for each

4. **Roll** balls in sugar and place on ungreased cookie sheets

5. **Bake** at 375 degrees for 10 to 12 minutes until golden brown

6. **Remove** from oven

7. **Top** each cookie immediately with a candy kiss, pressing down firmly so cookie cracks around edge
