## Nachos (Hors d'Oeuvres)

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 12 tortillas
- 1 10-ounce can jalapeno peppers
- 1 15-ounce can refried beans
- Â½ pound sliced cheddar cheese
- deep fat
- salt

## Instructions

1. **Cut** tortillas into quarters

2. **Fry** tortillas in deep fat until very crisp; drain

3. **Spread** refried beans on each tortilla quarter

4. **Cut** jalapeno peppers crosswise into circles

5. **Place** one round slice of jalapeno pepper on top of the refried beans, then cover with a triangle of cheese

6. **Place** in oven and heat at 350 degrees until cheese is melted

7. **Season** with salt to taste


###### *source: grandma's recipe, originally from "Old El Paso Don Ramon's Fiesta Foods"*
