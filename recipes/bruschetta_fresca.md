## Bruschetta Fresca

#### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 2 minutes | Makes 6 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 3 fresh garlic cloves
- 1 teaspoon balsamic vinegar
- 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
- 1 8-ounce container fresh mozzarella cheese, drained
- 1 ½ cups pre-diced fresh tomatoes
- 1 teaspoon seasoned salt
- ¼ teaspoon black pepper
- 1 baguette
- 5 fresh basil leaves

## Instructions

1. **Place** oven rack 6 inches from heat source, then preheat on broil

2. **Crush** garlic, using a garlic press, into a medium bowl

3. **Use** knife to remove garlic from bottom of press

4. **Stir in** vinegar and olive oil

5. **Cut** cheese into small, bite-size pieces; stir into garlic mixture with tomatoes, seasoned salt, and pepper

6. **Slice** bread into ½-inch slices; place on baking sheet

7. **Broil** 1 minute per side until lightly toasted

8. **Top** bread with tomato mixture

9. **Slice** rinsed basil into thin strips; sprinkle over top of bread
