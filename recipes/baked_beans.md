# Baked Beans

#### Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 cans pork and beans
- ½ teaspoon dry mustard
- ¼ cup brown sugar
- ½ cup ketchup
- 1 small onion
- bacon strips

## Instructions

1. **Mix** pork and beans, dry mustard, sugar, ketchup, and onion together

2. **Top** with bacon strips

3. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 30 minutes
