# Chicken Divan Pot Pie

### Prep Time: 30 minutes | Cook Time: 35 minutes | Makes 6 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 frozen regular pie crusts
- 3 tablespoons butter
- 3 tablespoons flour
- ¼ teaspoon pepper
- ½ cup chicken broth
- ¼ cup milk
- 4 ounces american cheese, shredded
- 2 cups chicken, cooked and diced
- 1 10-ounce package frozen chopped broccoli, thawed and drained well

## Instructions

1. **Preheat** oven to 425 degrees

2. **Place** a cookie sheet in the oven to preheat it

3. **Combine** butter, flour, and pepper in a medium saucepan

4. **Cook** over low heat until mixture becomes thick and bubbly

5. **Gradually stir in** broth and milk; stir until mixture begins to thicken

6. **Add** cheese; stir until melted

7. **Stir in** chicken and broccoli

8. **Pour** mixture into a pie shell

9. **Remove** second crust from pie pan; center frozen crust upright on top of filled pie

10. **Let sit** for 10-20 minutes and crimp edges together

11. **Cut** 2 to 3 slits in the top crust

12. **Place** pie on preheated cookie sheet

13. **Bake** at 425 degrees for 30-35 minutes, or until crust is brown and filling is bubbly
