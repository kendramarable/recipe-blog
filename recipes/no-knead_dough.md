#  No-Knead Dough

### Prep Time: 90 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Makes 16-24 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 packages active dry yeast
- 1 ¼ cups warm water
- ¼ cup butter, softened
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1 (4-serving) package egg custard mix
- 3 to 3 ⅓ cups all-purpose flour

## Instructions

1. **Soften** yeast in water in a large mixing bowl

2. **Add** butter, salt, and dry custard mix; stir until dissolved

3. **Gradually add** flour, mixing well

4. **Knead** dough on lightly floured surface 12 times

5. **Roll out** dough on lightly floured surface to make a 20x8 inch rectangle

6. **Cut** in half lengthwise, making two 20x4 inch strips

7. **Crease** each strip lengthwise, slightly off-center, with a knife handle

8. **Fold** along the crease

9. **Gently pull and shape** each strip to 24 inches

10. **Cut** into twelve 2-inch or eight 3-inch slices

11. **Place** on greased cookie sheet

12. **Cover** and let rise in a warm place until light, 45 to 60 minutes

13. **Bake** at 400 degrees for 10 to 15 minutes

14. **Serve** hot


*source: grandma's recipe, originally from "Pillsbury Blue Ribbon Recipes: The Idea Flour"*
