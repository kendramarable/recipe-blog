# Chili Rellenos (Stuffed Chilies)

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 5 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 4-ounce cans whole green chili
- 1 10-ounce can tomatoes & green chili
- Â½ pound cheddar cheese
- 2 eggs
- flour
- shortening


## Instructions

1. **Stuff** chilies with cheese

2. **Beat** egg whites until stiff; fold in beaten egg yolks

3. **Dip** stuffed chilies into egg mixture, then roll in flour

4. **Fry** in hot shortening until golden brown on both sides

5. **Serve** immediately with preheated tomatoes & green chili spooned over top
