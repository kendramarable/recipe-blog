# Smokin' Salmon Pizza

### Prep Time: 25 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 14 ¾-ounce can or 2 7 ½-ounce cans Alaska canned salmon, drained and flaked
- 1 prepared pizza crust or Italian bread shell (12 inches or 16 ounces)
- 1 tablespoon olive oil
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 3-ounce package cream cheese, cut into chunks
- ⅓ cup red onion, cut into very thin slices
- 2 cups shredded smoked swiss or mozzarella cheese
- 2 tablespoons chopped fresh dill
- 1 tablespoon drained bottled capers (optional)

## Instructions

1. **Preheat** oven to 400 degrees

2. **Place** pizza crust or bread shell on a cookie sheet

3. **Combine** oil and garlic

4. **Brush** evenly over pizza crust

5. **Arrange** salmon and cream cheese over pizza

6. **Top** with red onion, cheese, dill, and if desired, capers

7. **Bake** at 400 degrees for 12-15 minutes, or until edges of crust are crisp and cheese is melted

8. **Cut** into wedges
