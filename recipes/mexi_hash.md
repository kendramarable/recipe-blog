## Mexi Hash

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Makes 3 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 4 slices bacon
- ½ pound ground beef
- ½ cup chopped onion
- 1 can spanish rice
- ¼ cup water
- 1 teaspoon salt


## Instructions

1. **Cook** bacon in skillet over medium heat until crisp

2. **Remove** bacon; drain and crumble

3. **Add** ground beef and onion to a tablespoon of reserved bacon drippings

4. **Brown**, then stir in rice, water, salt, and crumbled bacon

5. **Heat** until steaming


###### *source: grandma's recipe, originally from "Old El Paso Don Ramon's Fiesta Foods"*
