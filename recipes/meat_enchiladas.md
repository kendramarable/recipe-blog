## Meat Enchiladas

#### Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 10-ounce cans enchilada sauce
- 12 tortillas
- 2 tablespoons shortening
- 2 medium onions, chopped
- ½ pound shredded cheddar cheese
- 1 can ripe olives, chopped
- 1 ½ cups cooked beef or chicken

## Instructions

1. **Fry** tortillas, one at a time, in 2 tablespoons of hot shortening

2. **Drain**, then lay in a shallow casserole dish (9x14 inch)

3. **Cover** with enchilada sauce, and top with grated cheese

4. **Heat** in oven at 350 degrees until hot and cheese is melted


###### *source: grandma's recipe, originally from "Old El Paso Recipes for Enchiladas"*
