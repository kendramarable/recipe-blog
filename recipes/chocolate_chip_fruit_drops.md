## Chocolate Chip Fruit Drops

#### Prep Time: 25 minutes | Cook Time: 12 minutes | Makes 84 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 ⅓ cups flour
- 1 teaspoon baking soda
- ¾ teaspoon salt
- ¾ teaspoon ground nutmeg
- ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
- 1 cup whole candied cherries
- ½ pound unsalted butter, room temperature
- ⅔ cup granulated sugar
- ⅓ cup light brown sugar
- ½ teaspoon vanilla extract
- 2 teaspoons rum or brandy extract
- 2 eggs
- 1 cup chocolate chips
- 1 ½ cups pecan pieces
- 1 cup diced candied orange peel

## Instructions

1. **Preheat** oven to 325 degrees

2. **Combine** flour, baking soda, salt, and spices

3. **Cut** cherries in half

4. **Beat** butter and sugars until fluffy

5. **Beat in** extracts and the eggs

6. **Beat in** flour mixture

7. **Stir in** cherries, chocolate chips, pecans, and orange peel

8. **Drop** dough by rounded teaspoonfuls on ungreased baking sheets, about 2 inches apart

9. **Bake** at 325 degrees until edges just start to brown, about 12 minutes

10. **Let stand** on baking sheets for 1 minute, then remove
