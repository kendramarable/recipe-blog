# Huevos Rancheros (Mexican Eggs)

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 10-ounce can tomatoes & green chili
- 12 tortillas
- 1 medium onion
- 1 clove garlic
- 4 eggs
- ½ teaspoon sugar
- ¼ teaspoon oregano
- shortening


## Instructions

1. **Saute** onion and garlic clove until limp

2. **Add** tomatoes & green chili, salt and pepper to taste, sugar, and oregano

3. **Simmer** for 15 minutes

4. **Dip** tortillas in hot shortening and spoon sauce on tortillas

5. **Make** four stacks of three tortillas each per serving

6. **Fry** eggs and place on top of tortilla stacks; cover with more sauce


###### *source: grandma's recipe, originally from "Old El Paso Mexican Food Favorites"*
