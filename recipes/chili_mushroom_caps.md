# Chili Mushroom Caps

### Prep Time: 50 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Makes 6 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 4 slices bacon
- 24 medium-sized mushrooms (1 ½ pounds)
- 1 tablespoon butter
- ¼ cup onion, finely chopped
- 1 clove garlic, finely chopped
- ⅓ cup sweet red pepper, finely chopped
- 2-3 pickled jalapeno peppers, seeded and finely chopped
- ½ cup shredded cheddar cheese
- ¼ cup fresh parsley, chopped

## Instructions

1. **Cook** bacon in skillet until almost crisp

2. **Remove** bacon from skillet; drain fat from skillet

3. **Chop** bacon

4. **Remove** stems from mushrooms

5. **Finely chop** two-thirds of stems

6. **Cook** mushroom caps in boiling water for 1 minute

7. **Drain**, rinse under cold water, and drain on paper towel

8. **Heat** butter in a skillet

9. **Add** chopped mushrooms, onion, and garlic

10. **Cook** for 5 minutes

11. **Add** red pepper and jalapeno

12. **Cook** for 3 minutes; let cool

13. **Stir in** bacon, cheese, and parsley

14. **Spoon** mixture into mushroom caps

15. **Place** on a baking sheet

16. **Bake** in oven at 350 degrees for 10 minutes, or until cheese is melted
