# Lemon Icebox Pie

### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 0 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 can sweetened condensed milk
- 2 egg yolks
- Â½ cup lemon juice
- 1 8- or 9-inch graham cracker crust
- Cool Whip for topping

## Instructions

1. **Mix** egg yolks and condensed milk

2. **Stir in** lemon juice

3. **Pour** into pie crust

4. **Top** with Cool Whip

5. **Chill** until firm
