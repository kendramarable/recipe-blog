## Pumpkin Rum Bread Pudding

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 60 minutes | Makes 16 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 6 cups dry whole wheat bread cubes
- ½ cup finely chopped walnuts
- 3 slightly beaten eggs
- 1 can sweetened condensed milk
- 1 cup pumpkin
- ¾ cup packed brown sugar
- 2 teaspoons cinnamon
- ½ teaspoon nutmeg
- 2 cups milk
- ¼ cup rum
- ¼ cup margarine, melted
- 2 teaspoons vanilla
- whipped cream

## Instructions

1. **Combine** bread and nuts in bottom of a 12x7x2 inch baking dish

2. **Stir** together eggs, condensed milk, pumpkin, brown sugar, cinnamon, and nutmeg

3. **Add** milk gradually

4. **Add** rum, butter, and vanilla

5. **Pour** over bread mixture

6. **Place** baking dish in a larger pan and pour hot water to 1 inch level around the baking dish

7. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 50 to 60 minutes, or until knife inserted in center comes out clean

8. **Serve** with whipped cream
