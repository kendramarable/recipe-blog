## Sausage Balls

#### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 60 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 pound ground sausage
- 2 cups biscuit baking mix
- 12 ounces cheddar cheese, shredded

## Instructions

1. **Combine** sausage, biscuit mix, and cheese together in a large bowl

2. **Mix** with hands until well combined

3. **Roll** mixture into balls

4. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 25 minutes or until sausage is cooked through
