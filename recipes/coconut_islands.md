# Coconut Islands

### Prep Time: 25 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Makes 42 Servings

## Ingredients: Cake
 
- 3 envelopes (3 ounces) pre-melted unsweetened chocolate
- 1 teaspoon instant coffee
- ½ cup soft butter
- 1 cup firmly packed brown sugar
- 1 egg
- ⅔ cup sour cream
- 2 cups self-rising flour
- 1 cup flaked coconut

## Ingredients: Chocolate Frosting

- 2 envelopes (2 ounces) pre-melted unsweetened chocolate
- ¼ cup sour cream
- 2 cups confectioners sugar, sifted


## Instructions: Cake

1. **Combine** chocolate, coffee, butter, brown sugar, egg, sour cream, flour, and ⅓ cup coconut in a large mixer bowl

2. **Mix** on lowest speed until well-blended

3. **Drop** dough by rounded teaspoonfuls onto greased cookie sheets

4. **Bake** at 375 degrees for 12-15 minutes

5. **Frost** with chocolate frosting while warm

6. **Sprinkle** with the remaining coconut

## Instructions: Chocolate Frosting

1. **Combine** chocolate, sour cream, and confectioners sugar in mixing bowl

2. **Blend** well, thinning with a few drops of additional sour cream if necessary
