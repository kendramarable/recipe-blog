# Praline Cheesecake

#### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 65 minutes | Makes 12 Servings

## Ingredients: Crust
 
- 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
- 3 tablespoons margarine, melted
- 3 tablespoons sugar

## Ingredients: Filling

- 3 8-ounce packages cream cheese, softened
- 1 ¼ cups dark brown sugar, packed
- 3 eggs
- 2 tablespoons flour
- ½ cup chopped pecans
- 1 ½ teaspoons vanilla

## Instructions: Crust

1. **Combine** graham cracker crumbs, margarine, and sugar

2. **Press** into the bottom of an 8-inch springform pan

3. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 10 minutes

## Instructions: Filling

1. **Beat** cream cheese and sugar until fluffy

2. **Beat in** eggs, one at a time, then sift in the flour

3. **Add** pecans and vanilla, then pour into crust

4. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 55 minutes

5. **Let** cake cool in pan

6. **Transfer** to plate and chill for at least 3 hours
