# Frozen Neapolitan Delight

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 0 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 12-ounce package frozen pound cake
- 1 pint neapolitan brick ice cream
- 1 4 Â½-ounce frozen whipped topping, thawed
- 14 pecan halves
- 8 maraschino cherries
- heavy duty aluminum foil

## Instructions

1. **Cut** pound cake lengthwise into 2 equal layers

2. **Place** one layer on an 18-inch square sheet of aluminum foil

3. **Cut** ice cream brick lengthwise into 2 equal slices; place on cake layer

4. **Top** with the second cake layer

5. **Frost** top and slices with whipped topping

6. **Decorate** with pecans and cherries

7. **Place** on foil in freezer for about 2 hours, or until topping is frozen hard

8. **Remove** from freezer

9. **Bring** foil up, double fold over top, and fold in ends

10. **Return** to freezer until ready to serve
