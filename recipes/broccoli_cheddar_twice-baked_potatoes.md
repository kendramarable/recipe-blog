#  Broccoli Cheddar Twice-Baked Potatoes

### Prep Time: 25 minutes | Cook Time: 75 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 4 medium baking potatoes
- 1 cup plain yogurt
- ½ teaspoon salt
- ¼ teaspoon pepper
- ⅛ teaspoon paprika
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 cup chopped broccoli
- 1 cup sliced mushrooms
- ¼ cup chopped pimentos, well-drained


## Instructions

1. **Wash** potatoes well, then dry and pierce several times with a fork

2. **Bake** at 400 degrees for 1 hour or until soft, then let cool

3. **Cut** potatoes in half lengthwise; carefully scoop potato pulp into bowl (leave ½ inch of pulp to keep the potato skins intact)

4. **Set** potato skins aside

5. **Add** yogurt, salt, pepper, paprika, and ½ cup cheddar cheese to pulp

6. **Mix** until light and fluffy, then set aside

7. **Coat** a skillet with oil; heat until hot

8. **Add** garlic, broccoli, mushrooms, and pimentos to skillet

9. **Cook** until tender

10. **Stir** vegetable mixture into potato mixture

11. **Stuff** potato skins with mixture

12. **Place** potatoes on an ungreased baking sheet

13. **Sprinkle** potatoes evenly with remaining ½ cup cheddar cheese

14. **Bake** potatoes at 375 degrees for 10 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly


*source: grandma's recipe, originally from Dannon*
