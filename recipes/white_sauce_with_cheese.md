# White Sauce With Cheese

#### Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 10 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 tablespoons butter
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 cup milk
- 1 cup mild cheese, grated
- Â½ teaspoon dry mustard


## Instructions

1. **Melt** butter over low heat

2. **Add** flour and blend over low heat for 3-5 minutes

3. **Slowly stir in** the milk until smooth and hot

4. **Reduce** the heat and stir in the cheese

5. **Season** with dry mustard

6. **Stir** until cheese is melted
