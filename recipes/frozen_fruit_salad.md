#  Frozen Fruit Salad

#### Prep Time: 45 minutes | Cook Time: 0 minutes | Makes Several Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 4 cans (1 pound, 4 ounce) crushed pineapple
- 2 cans (1 pound) sliced peaches
- 2 cans (1 pound, 4 ounce) white seedless grapes
- 1 ½ cups maraschino cherries
- 30 marshmallows
- 2 teaspoons crystallized ginger
- 1 envelope unflavored gelatin
- ¼ cup cold water
- 1 cup orange juice
- ¼ cup lemon juice
- 2 ½ cups sugar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 cups pecans, chopped
- 2 quarts heavy cream, whipped
- 3 cups mayonnaise

## Instructions

1. **Drain** fruit; save 1 ½ cups pineapple juice

2. **Cut** peaches in ½ inch cubes, grapes in halves, cherries in eights, and marshmallows in fourths

3. **Combine** fruit, marshmallows, and finely chopped ginger

4. **Soften** gelatin in cold water

5. **Heat** pineapple juice to boiling

6. **Add** gelatin; stir to dissolve

7. **Add** orange and lemon juices, sugar, and salt; stir to dissolve

8. **Chill**; when mixture starts to thicken, add fruit mixture and nuts

9. **Fold** in whipped cream and mayonnaise

10. **Spoon** into quart cartons (paper, plastic, or metal)

11. **Cover** and freeze

12. **Thaw and serve** by cutting into 1-inch slices and serving salad on lettuce, garnishing with cherries
