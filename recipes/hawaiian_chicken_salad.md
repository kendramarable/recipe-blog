# Hawaiian Chicken Salad

#### Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 0 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 3 cans chicken, drained
- low fat salad dressing or mayonnaise
- 1 small can crushed pineapple, drained
- 1 small can mandarin oranges, drained
- 1 cup pecans
- 1 small bunch red seedless grapes, cut in half
- 1 cup mini marshmallows (optional)


## Instructions

1. **Mix** ingredients well; let sit overnight

2. **Serve** on lettuce and garnish with fresh fruit
