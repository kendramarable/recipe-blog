# Mexican Chicken

### Prep Time: 50 minutes | Cook Time: 40 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 4 boneless, skinless chicken breast halves
- 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 1 ½ cups onion, chopped
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 green pepper, chopped
- 2 teaspoons chili powder
- ¼ teaspoon ground cloves
- 1 28-ounce can tomatoes, cut up and juiced (do not drain)
- ⅓ cup raisins
- salt and freshly ground pepper
- 4 tablespoons dry sherry, optional
- ½ cup green or black sliced olives, optional

## Instructions

1. **Rinse** chicken and pat dry

2. **Cut** chicken into 1-inch pieces

3. **Salt and pepper** chicken pieces; set aside

4. **Place** oil in a large saucepan and heat over medium-high heat

5. **Add** chicken and stir; cook until browned

6. **Remove** and set aside

7. **Reduce** heat to medium and add onion, garlic, and green pepper

8. **Stir and cook** until soft, about 3-4 minutes

9. **Add** chili powder, cloves, tomatoes, raisins, and chicken

10. **Add** sherry, if desired

11. **Stir** well, cover pan, and reduce heat to low

12. **Simmer** for 30-40 minutes

13. **Add** olives before serving, if desired

14. **Serve** over rice with a green salad
