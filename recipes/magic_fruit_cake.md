#  Magic Fruit Cake

#### Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 90 minutes | Makes 10 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 9-ounce package mince meat
- ½ cup water
- 1 cup walnut meats, coarsely chopped
- 2 3-ounce cans (¾ cup) mixed candied fruit, coarsely chopped
- 1 can (1 ½ cups) sweetened condensed milk
- 1 egg, beaten
- ¾ cup flour
- ½ teaspoon baking soda

## Instructions

1. **Break** mince meat into small pieces and put in 2-quart saucepan

2. **Add** water to saucepan

3. **Place** over heat and stir until lumps are thoroughly broken

4. **Boil** briskly for one minute, stirring constantly

5. **Remove** from heat and cool

6. **Add** nuts, candied fruit, condensed milk, and egg; blend well

7. **Stir in** flour and baking soda, until just blended

8. **Pour** mixture into 9x4x3 inch loaf pan which has been greased, waxed paper lined, and greased again

9. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 1 hour and 30 minutes, or until center springs back when lightly touched with finger and top is golden brown
