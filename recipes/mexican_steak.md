# Mexican Steak

#### Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 25 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 pound round steak
- 2 tablespoons shortening
- 2 large potatoes, sliced
- 2 4-ounce cans whole green chili, cut in strips
- 2 small onions, sliced
- 1 cup canned tomatoes
- 1 cup red chili puree
- 1 clove garlic, chopped fine
- 1 tablespoon vinegar
- 1 teaspoon pepper
- 1 tablespoon flour


## Instructions

1. **Rub** vinegar on both sides of meat

2. **Pound in** garlic, salt, pepper, and flour

3. **Cut** meat into small pieces

4. **Brown** in the shortening

5. **Cook** until meat is tender

6. **Add** vegetables and season to taste

7. **Add** red chili puree and simmer until vegetables are tender


###### *source: grandma's recipe, originally from "Old El Paso Don Ramon Says Enjoy a Real Mexican Dinner at Home"*
