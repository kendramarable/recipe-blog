# Vanilla Frosting

### Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 0 minutes | Makes Several Servings

## Ingredients
 
- ¼ cup Crisco shortening, melted
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 2 teaspoons vanilla
- 3 cups confectioners sugar, sift if lumpy
- ¼ cup milk

## Instructions

1. **Mix** shortening, salt, vanilla, and about a third of the sugar in a bowl

2. **Add** milk and the remaining sugar alternately; mix until smooth and creamy

3. **Add** more sugar to thicken, or add more milk to thin frosting, if needed


*source: grandma's recipe, originally from Crisco*
