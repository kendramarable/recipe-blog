## Date Squares

#### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes | Makes 12 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 cup plus 2 tablespoons of unsifted cake flour
- 1 teaspoon baking powder
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 1 cup sugar
- 2 eggs, well-beaten
- ¼ cup butter or margarine
- 1 tablespoon hot water
- 2 teaspoons grated orange rind (optional)
- 2 cups dates, finely cut
- 1 cup chopped nuts


## Instructions

1. **Mix** flour with baking powder and salt

2. **Gradually beat** sugar into eggs

3. **Add** butter, water, and orange rind

4. **Stir in** dates and nuts

5. **Gradually add** flour mixture, beating well

6. **Spread** batter evenly in a greased 13x9 inch pan

7. **Bake** at 325 degrees for 25-30 minutes

8. **Let cool** then cut into bars

9. **Sprinkle with or roll in** confectioners sugar, if desired
