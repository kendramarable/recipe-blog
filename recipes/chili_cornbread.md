# Chili Cornbread

#### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes | Makes 9 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 package cornbread mix (or homemade)
- 1 9-ounce can cream-style corn
- 1 cup sharp cheese, grated
- 2 tablespoons bacon drippings



## Instructions

1. **Prepare** cornbread mix

2. **Add** cream-style corn and bacon drippings to mix

3. **Spoon** half the batter into greased 9x9x2 inch pan

4. **Lay** chili on batter and sprinkle with half the cheese

5. **Top** with remaining batter and cheese

6. **Bake** at 450 degrees until done, about 30 minutes
