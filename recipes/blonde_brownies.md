# Blonde Brownies

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes | Makes 24 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 cups flour
- ⅔ cup butter
- 2 cups brown sugar
- 2 eggs
- 2 teaspoons vanilla
- 1 cup chocolate chips
- ½ cup nuts

## Instructions

1. **Melt** butter

2. **Add** sugar to melted butter

3. **Blend in** eggs and vanilla

4. **Add** flour; mix well

5. **Add** chocolate chips and nuts

6. **Spread** in a 13x9 inch pan

7. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 30 minutes
