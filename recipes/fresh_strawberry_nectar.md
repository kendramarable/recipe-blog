## Fresh Strawberry Nectar

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 0 minutes | Makes Several Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 pint strawberries, rinsed and hulled
- 3 tablespoons sugar
- 2 tablespoons lemon juice
- 3 cups boiled water
- 3 cups cold water
- 1 large orange's peel
- 1 large orange's juice
- crushed ice
- whole berries (optional)


## Instructions

1. **Put** berries, sugar, lemon juice, and boiled water in an electric blender

2. **Cover and blend** at low speed for 20 seconds

3. **Strain** into a 2-quart pitcher

4. **Add** cold water, orange peel, and orange juice

5. **Cover and chill** for several hours

6. **Serve** over crushed ice and garnish with whole strawberries
