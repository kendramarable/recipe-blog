# Stir Fried Pork

#### Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 55 minutes | Makes 6 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 ½ pounds pork shoulder
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons water
- corn starch
- ¼ cup vegetable oil
- 1 clove garlic, minced
- 1 large onion
- 2 carrots, sliced diagonally
- 2 stalks celery
- 1 green pepper
- 1 yellow squash
- ¼ pound green beans
- water chestnuts
- ½ cup chicken broth
- ¼ cup soy sauce
- ¼ pound mushrooms
- ¼ pound fresh spinach

## Instructions

1. **Dip** pork into a mixture of egg and water in a small bowl

2. **Coat** with corn starch on wax paper

3. **Heat** oil with garlic in a wok

4. **Add** pork to wok

5. **Brown** pork until golden

6. **Remove** pork and keep warm

7. **Reheat** oil in the wok

8. **Stir fry** onion slices for 2 minutes

9. **Add** carrots, celery, peppers, squash, and green beans

10. **Stir fry** for 3 minutes

11. **Return** pork to pan with chicken broth and soy sauce

12. **Bring** to a boil, then lower heat

13. **Cover and steam** for 10 minutes

14. **Add** mushrooms, spinach, and water chestnuts

15. **Cook** for 5 minutes, or until tender and crisp

16. **Serve** over rice
