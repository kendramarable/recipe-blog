## Chicken Parmesan

#### Prep Time: 35 minutes | Cook Time: 6 minutes | Makes 6 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 egg
- 2 tablespoons parmesan cheese, grated
- 1 cup Italian-flavored dry breadcrumbs
- 6 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves (about 2 pounds), lightly pounded
- 4 tablespoons olive or vegetable oil, divided
- 6 ounces shiitake mushrooms, sliced
- 2 tablespoons dry red wine
- 1 26-ounce jar marinara sauce
- 8 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, sliced
- 1 tablespoon water
- fresh basil leaves or parsley

## Instructions

1. **Preheat** oven to 375 degrees

2. **Beat** egg with 1 tablespoon of water

3. **Stir** parmesan cheese into breadcrumbs

4. **Dip** chicken in beaten egg, then coat with breadcrumb mixture

5. **Heat** 2 tablespoons of the oil in a skillet over medium heat

6. **Brown** chicken for 3-4 minutes per side, until almost cooked through and golden brown

7. **Place** in a single layer in a lightly-greased baking pan

8. **Wipe out** the skillet

9. **Heat** remaining oil and cook mushrooms for 3 minutes, stirring often, until cooked through

10. **Stir** wine into marinara sauce; spoon over cutlets

11. **Top** wish mushrooms

12. **Bake** at 375 degrees for 10 minutes

13. **Top** each cutlet with a slice of mozzarella cheese and bake for 10 minutes longer

14. **Garnish** with basil or parsley
