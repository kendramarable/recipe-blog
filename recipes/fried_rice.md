## Fried Rice

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 15 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 3 cups cold cooked rice
- 3 eggs
- ¼ teaspoon salt
- ½ tablespoon water
- 2 tablespoons oil
- 2 scallions, cut into ½-inch pieces
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce

## Instructions

1. **Separate** the grains of rice with a fork

2. **Lightly beat** the eggs, salt, and water

3. **Heat** the oil in a wok

4. **Add** scallions and stir fry for 30 seconds

5. **Add** rice and stir fry until heated through (each grain is coated with oil)

6. **Pour in** the egg mixture and continue stirring until the egg is nearly set

7. **Add** soy sauce and stir until well-combined

8. **Serve** immediately
