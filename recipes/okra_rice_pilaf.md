# Okra Rice Pilaf

#### Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 45 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 cups long grain rice
- 6 slices bacon, chopped
- 2 medium onions, chopped
- 2 cans stewed tomatoes
- 2 cups okra, thinly sliced
- 2 teaspoons salt
- Â¼ teaspoon pepper

## Instructions

1. **Cook** rice in a skillet

2. **Cook** chopped bacon; put aside

3. **Saute** onions in the hot bacon fat, stirring until tender (about 5 minutes)

4. **Add** tomatoes and okra

5. **Cook**, stirring until most liquid is evaporated

6. **Add** salt and pepper

7. **Drain** rice, if necessary, and combine with tomato mixture in top of double boiler

8. **Cook** covered over boiling water, about 20 minutes

9. **Add** bacon before serving
