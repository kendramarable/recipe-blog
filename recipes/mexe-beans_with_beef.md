#  Mexe-Beans With Beef

### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 20 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 pound ground beef
- ½ cup chopped onions
- 1 15-ounce can mexe-beans
- 1 10-ounce can mild enchilada sauce
- 1 teaspoon salt
- ½ teaspoon chili powder
- ¼ teaspoon garlic powder



## Instructions

1. **Cook** ground beef and onion in large skillet over medium heat

2. **Drain**, then add remaining ingredients

3. **Cook** over low heat for 20 minutes


*source: grandma's recipe, originally from "Old El Paso Don Ramon's Fiesta Foods"*
