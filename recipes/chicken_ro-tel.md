# Chicken Ro-Tel

#### Prep Time: 20 minutes | Cook Time: 40 minutes | Makes 6 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 2 pounds chicken breast
- 1 can cream of chicken
- 1 can cream of mushroom
- 1 can chicken broth
- 1 can Ro-Tel tomatoes
- 1 medium onion, chopped
- nacho chips
- cheddar cheese, grated

## Instructions

1. **Cook and debone** chicken; cut into strips

2. **Mix** soups, broth, tomatoes, and onion in a saucepan and simmer

3. **Layer** nacho chips, chicken, and soup mixture in a greased baking dish

4. **Top** with grated cheese

5. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 30-40 minutes
