# Double Pistachio Cake

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 50 minutes | Makes 12 Servings

## Ingredients

- 1 package yellow cake mix
- 1 package pistachio instant pudding
- 3 eggs
- ½ cup oil
- 1 cup club soda (or water)
- ½ cup chopped nuts


## Instructions

1. **Blend** all of the ingredients together in a mixing bowl

2. **Beat** with a mixer on medium for 2 minutes

3. **Place** mixture in a 13 x 9 inch pan

4. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 50 minutes
