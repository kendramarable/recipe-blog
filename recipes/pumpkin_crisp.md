## Pumpkin Crisp

#### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 60 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 15-ounce can pumpkin puree
- 1 cup evaporated milk
- 1 cup sugar
- 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
- Â½ teaspoon cinnamon
- 1 package butter golden cake mix
- 1 cup chopped pecans
- 1 cup butter, melted

## Instructions

1. **Stir together** the pumpkin, milk, sugar, vanilla, and cinnamon in a bowl

2. **Pour** mixture into a 13x9-inch baking dish

3. **Pour** cake mix evenly over pumpkin mixture

4. **Sprinkle** pecans evenly over cake mix

5. **Drizzle** melted butter over pecans

6. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 1 hour or until golden brown
