# Holiday Ham and Cheese Ball

#### Prep Time: 15 minutes | Cook Time: 0 minutes | Makes Several Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 1 3-ounce package cream cheese
- 1 4 ½-ounce can deviled ham 
- 2 tablespoons scallions, finely chopped
- 3 tablespoons chopped green chilies
- ½ cup chopped walnuts


## Instructions

1. **Combine** cheddar cheese, cream cheese, deviled ham, scallions, and chilies in a small bowl

2. **Mix** well

3. **Shape** mixture into a ball

4. **Roll in** the nuts

5. **Chill**; serve with crackers
