## Green Chili Corn Casserole

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 30 minutes | Makes 8 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 16-ounce can cream-style corn
- 1 4-ounce can chopped green chili
- 2 tablespoons flour
- 1 Â½ tablespoons sugar
- 2 eggs
- butter


## Instructions

1. **Mix** the corn, green chili, flour, and sugar in a large mixing bowl

2. **Beat** in two eggs into the mixing bowl

3. **Place** in a casserole dish; dot with butter

4. **Bake** at 350 degrees for 30 minutes
