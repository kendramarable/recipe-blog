#  Zesty Meat Loaf

#### Prep Time: 10 minutes | Cook Time: 60 minutes | Makes 4 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 ½ pounds ground beef
- 1 cup soft bread crumbs
- 1 10-ounce can tomatoes & green chili
- 3 tablespoons instant minced onion
- 1 ¼ teaspoons salt

## Instructions

1. **Preheat** oven to 375 degrees

2. **Mix** all ingredients thoroughly in a large bowl

3. **Shape** mixture, with wet hands, into a loaf in a shallow baking pan

4. **Bake** for 1 hour


###### *source: grandma's recipe, originally from "Old El Paso Don Ramon's Fiesta Foods"*
