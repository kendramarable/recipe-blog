# Carnation's 5 Minute Fudge

#### Prep Time: 5 minutes | Cook Time: 5 minutes | Makes 20 Servings

## Ingredients
 
- 1 small can (⅔ cup) undiluted Carnation evaporated milk
- 1 ⅔ cups sugar
- ½ teaspoon salt
- 16 medium diced marshmallows (or 1 ½ cups)
- 1 ½ cups chocolate chips
- 1 teaspoon vanilla
- ½ cup chopped nuts


## Instructions

1. **Combine** evaporated milk, sugar, and salt in saucepan over medium heat

2. **Heat to boiling** and cook for 5 minutes, stirring constantly

3. **Remove** from heat

4. **Add** diced marshmallows, chocolate chips, vanilla, and chopped nuts

5. **Stir** for 1-2 minutes, or until marshmallows melt

6. **Pour** into buttered 9-inch square pan

7. **Cool**, then cut into squares
